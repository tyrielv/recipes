# Almond Macaroon Tarts

*Adapted from a family cookbook*

Preheat oven to 325º

## Ingredients

### Crust

- 1 stick softened butter
- 1/4 C sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 C flour

### Filling

- 1 egg
- 1/4 C sugar
- 2/3 C almonds, finely ground (or made into almond butter)
- 1/4 tsp almond extract
- (optional) jam

## Directions

Cream together butter and sugar. Stir in egg, vanilla, and flour, until smooth. Spoon into greased mini-muffin pan. Press thin along sides and bottom (wet hands often to keep from sticking). Put in fridge while making filling.

Beat eggs and sugar until well blended. Mix in almonds and extract. Optionally put 1/4 tsp of jam into bottom of each muffin cup. Fill each cup with the filling.

Bake 25-30 minutes until slightly browned.

## Notes

* For just macaroons, to try next time without crust:
* Use 2 egg whites instead of 1 whole egg for filling. Beat to soft peaks
* Bake on parchment paper.
