# Barley Lentil Soup

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 onion, coarsely chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 large carrot, chopped
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1/4 tsp oregano
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- salt to taste
- 4 c chicken broth
- few dashes of Worcestershire sauce
- 1 (14.5oz) can diced tomatoes
- 1 c brown or green lentils
- 1/4 c barley, pearled
- cumin and dried parsley flakes to taste

## Directions

In a large saucepan or soup pot, heat oil over medium heat. Sauté onion, garlic, celery, thyme, oregano, and pepper until onions are softened, about 5 minutes.

Add chicken broth, lentils, barley, diced tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce, cumin, and parsley to the pot and bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until barley and lentils are tender, about 40 minutes.

If it seems as though the liquid is boiling down too much, add a cup or two more of chicken broth.

Serve with a small scoop of sour cream or plain yogurt if desired. (I actually like to stir a little bit of sour cream into my bowl of soup for a more creamy texture.)
