# Brazilian Cheese Balls

*Adapted from [Our Best Bites](http://ourbestbites.com/2011/02/quick-brazilian-cheese-rolls-pao-de-queijo-2/)*

## Ingredients

- 1 C tapioca flour (also called tapioca starch, cassava flour)
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 egg
- 1/2 C milk
- 1/4 C oil
- 1/2 C finely grated cheese (e.g. swiss, parmesan, sharp cheddar)

## Directions

Mix everything but cheese until smooth. Mix in cheese. Batter will be very runny. Spoon into greased mini muffin pan and bake at 400º for 12-15 minutes, not long enough to brown on top. (Regular size muffin pan works too, just bake 3-5 minutes longer.)

Makes 16-24 mini muffins, depending on how full you fill them.
