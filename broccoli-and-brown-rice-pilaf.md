# Broccoli and Brown Rice Pilaf

## Ingredients

- 1 c uncooked brown rice
- 2 1/4 c chicken or vegetable broth
- 2 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 c chopped fresh broccoli
- 1/4 c slivered almonds
- 1/4 c unsalted sunflower kernels
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper

## Directions

In a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray, sauté rice until lightly browned. Add the broth, rosemary, and garlic; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 40 minutes or until rice is almost tender.

Stir in broccoli, almonds, sunflower kernels, salt, and pepper. Cover and cook 3-5 minutes or longer until rice is tender and broccoli is crisp-tender. Fluff with a fork before serving!
