# Cheese Dumplings (for Tomato Soup)

*Recipe from "Have Apron, Will Travel" (family heirloom cookbook).*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 and 1/2 C flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp shortening
- 1/4 c grated cheddar cheese
- 3/4 c milk

---

## Directions

Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.

Cut in shortening.

Stir in cheese and milk.

Drop spoonfuls of dough in simmering [Tomato Soup](tomato-soup-1-quart.md).

Cook 10 minutes uncovered, then cover and let simmer for 10 more minutes.
