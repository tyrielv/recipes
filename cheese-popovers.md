# Cheese Popovers

*Adapted from "100% Natural Lard: The Lost Art of Cooking with Your Grandmother's Secret Ingredient"*

**Notes for next time: Try 1 1/2 batch for 12 muffins, and double the salt**

## Ingredients

* Lard for greasing pan
* 1 C flour
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1 C milk
* 1/4 C grated cheddar cheese
* 1 Tbl melted butter
* 2 eggs

## Directions

Preheat oven to 450ºF. Put 1/2 tsp lard in each cup that you'll use of a muffin or popover pan, and put the pan in the oven while you mix the dough and it preheats.

Whisk flour and salt together in a bowl. Add milk, cheese, butter, and eggs. Beat with an electric mixer on low for 2 minutes.

When oven is preheated, remove pan and fill each cup ~2/3s full with batter. Bake 17/20 minutes for muffins/popovers, then reduce heat to 325ºF and continue baking for 10/15 minutes until puffed and golden brown on top.

Makes 9 muffins or 6 popovers (if you have a dedicated popover pan)
