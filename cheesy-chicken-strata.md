# Cheesy Chicken Strata

*Adapted from [The America's Test Kitchen Quick Family Cookbook](https://www.amazon.com/Americas-Kitchen-Quick-Family-Cookbook/dp/1933615990/)*

## Ingredients

- 1 onion, diced
- 4 Tbsp butter
- 6 cups cubed dried bread
- 1-2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
- 6 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups whole milk
- 1/4 tsp thyme
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 4 oz shredded sharp cheddar

## Directions

Sauté onion in butter over medium high heat until slightly browned, 5-10 minutes. Stir in dried bread cubes and remove from heat.

Whisk eggs and milk together in a bowl. Stir in thyme, salt, pepper, and cheese. Mix in the onions, chicken, and bread. Pour into a 9x13 pan or similar-sized casserole dish lined with parchment paper. Cover and refrigerate for at least several hours, up to overnight, to allow bread to absorb the egg mixture.

Place dish in cold oven and turn on to 350º. Bake for 45-60 minutes until cooked through.
