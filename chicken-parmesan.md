# Chicken Parmesan

*Adapted from [Cook's Illustrated](https://www.cooksillustrated.com/recipes/926-chicken-parmesan)*

## Ingredients

- Two large chicken breasts, or four small, cut/pounded into 1/4" thick cutlets
- 1 egg
- 1/2 C bread crumbs
- 1/2 oz Parmesan or similar cheese, grated
- 1/2 tsp salt, divided
- 1/4 C olive oil
- 3/4 C (6 oz) shredded mozzarella

## Directions

Mix bread crumbs, Parmesan, and 1/4 tsp salt in a wide bowl. Mix egg and 1/4 tsp salt in another bowl. One by one, dip the cutlets in the egg on both sides, let extra egg drip off, and coat in bread crumb mixture. Set on a wire rack over a cookie sheet.

Heat olive oil over medium high in a 12-inch skillet, until oil shimmers. Add chicken cutlets and fry for 3 minutes on each side, until golden brown. (May need to do 2 batches depending on size.) Transfer back to wire rack.

Top with shredded mozzarella. Broil on the wire rack and cookie sheet 4 inches from heating element for 3-5 minutes, until cheese is melted and starting to brown.

Serve with spaghetti and sauce.
