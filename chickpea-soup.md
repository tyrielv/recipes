# Chickpea Soup

## Ingredients

- 2 c chickpeas, cooked (or 2 cans of chickpeas)
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp minced garlic
- 2 leeks, sliced thin (we just used green onions)
- 1 (14.5oz) can diced tomatoes
- 1 tsp dried rosemary
- 1 tbsp dried basil
- 1 tbsp parsley
- 6 c broth or stock
- 1/3 lb small pasta (e.g. orzo or similar)

## Directions

Heat 3 tbsp olive oil in a large saucepan. Saute 2 tbsp minced garlic and 2 sliced leeks until garlic is golden brown. Add 1 (14.5 oz) can tomatoes and 1 tsp rosemary; simmer 3-4 minutes stirring constantly. Stir in 2 cups cooked chickpeas, 1 tbsp basil, 1 tbsp parsley, and 6 cups broth. Bring to a high boil. Add 1/3 lb pasta and cook uncovered until al dente.
