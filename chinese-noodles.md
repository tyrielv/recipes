# Chinese Noodles

*Source: <https://natashaskitchen.com/chicken-chow-mein/>*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, tofu*, or other meat of your choice cut into bite-sized pieces
- 3 tbsp oil
- 14 oz chow mein or lo mein noodles
- 2 cups cabbage, chopped
- 1-2 large carrots, julienned
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 batch green onions

**Sauce**

- 6 tbsp oyster sauce
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 3 tbsp sesame oil
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp granulated sugar

---

## Directions

1. In a small mixing bowl, combine ingredients for sauce: oyster sauce, sesame oil, soy sauce, chicken broth, and cornstarch. If using, stir tofu into sauce now. Set aside.
2. Cook noodles according to package instructions, set aside.
3. Heat a large wok or pan on medium high. Add a bit of oil to the pan and cook chicken/meat until it's golden brown. Remove meat and set aside. If using tofu, preheat oil in wok and skip to the next step.
4. Add carrots and cabbage and sauté for a few minutes until veggies are slightly softened and cabbage is slightly translucent.
5. Add chicken back to the pan, followed by cooked noodles and pour the sauce right on top. Cook for about 2 minutes, distributing all that saucy goodness around evenly.
6. Add chopped green onions and remove from the heat. Serve hot!

---

## Notes

*Before making this recipe, Tofu can be pressed, cut into 1/4 - 1/2" cubes, and lightly baked at 300°F for 30 minutes, flipping halfway through, for a chewier texture closer to chicken.
