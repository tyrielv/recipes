# Chocolate Vegetable Muffins

*Adapted from <https://thenaturalnurturer.com/healthy-chocolate-muffins-with-veggies/>*

---

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 C unsweetened applesauce (or 1 C mashed overripe banana)
- 1 C grated carrots
- 1 C loosely packed spinach or kale
- 1/4 C oil
- 1/4 C honey
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 C whole wheat flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 C cocoa powder
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 C chocolate chips

---

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 ºF.
2. Puree eggs, fruit/veggies, and liquids until smooth.
3. Mix in dry ingredients, just until mixed.
4. Stir in chocolate chips.
5. Bake in muffin pan 20-22 minutes.
