# Coconut Curry Noodles

*Adapted from "The Fiber Fueled Cookbook" by Will Mulsiewicz*

---

## Ingredients

- 8 oz (half package) of rice noodles
- 14 oz extra firm tofu, cubed
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp chili powder
- 4 tsp tamari or soy sauce
- 2 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp cornstarch
- 1 bunch scallions, chopped
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- 2 C (1 small bunch) chopped kale
- 1 can coconut milk
- 1/2 C water
- 1/2 tsp salt, to taste
- 1/2 tsp MSG (optional)
- 2 Tbsp lime juice

---

## Directions

Soften noodles according to package directions. (Typically: Soak in boiling-hot water for 10 minutes, drain).

In a small bowl toss tofu with oil, turmeric, pepper, chili powder, soy sauce, curry powder, cornstarch, scallions, ginger, and garlic.

Cook in a large skillet over medium heat ~10 minutes (it's ok if it sticks).

Add kale, coconut milk, water, salt, and MSG. Bring to a simmer, scraping the bottom of the pan if anything stuck, then cover and cook 10 minutes.

Stir in rice noodles and lime juice. Continue cooking 2-5 minutes until liquid is mostly absorbed.

(Alternatively could be served over rice).
