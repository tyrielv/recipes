# Coleslaw with Italian Dressing

*Adapted from the top 5 google search results for "coleslaw with Italian dressing."*

---

## Ingredients

- 3/4 Cup vegetable oil
- 1/4 Cup red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp basil
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes
- 1/2 head cabbage, shredded
- 2-4 carrots, shredded

---

## Directions

Whisk together dressing ingredients.

Toss vegetables and dressing together until evenly mixed. Best if served chilled.
