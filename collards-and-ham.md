# Collards and Ham

*Adapted from <https://www.seriouseats.com/collard-greens-ham-hocks-recipe>*

---

## Ingredients

- ~4-6oz ham, chopped
- 12oz collard greens (about 1 bunch), stems removed and chopped.
- 1/2 an onion, diced, OR 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- 2 C chicken broth
- salt: 3/4 tsp for homemade broth, 1/4 tsp for bouillon/store-bought

---

## Directions

Combine all ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil on high heat. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for at least 30 minutes, up to 3 hours.
