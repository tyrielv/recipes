# Cottage Cheese Pancakes

*Source: <https://momsdish.com/recipe/177/cottage-cheese-pancakes>*

We tried these on our kids for a higher-protein breakfast, and the kids couldn't tell the difference between these and our usual pancakes!

---

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups cottage cheese
- 4 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1 cup flour
- 1/4 cup canola oil

---

## Directions

Combine all ingredients in a medium mixing bowl. For a smoother texture, we mixed everything used an immersion blender so the cottage cheese wouldn't be noticeable for our picky children.

Fry on both sides on a preheated griddle sprayed with nonstick spray.
