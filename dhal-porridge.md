# Dhal Porridge

*Adapted from <https://thewanderlustkitchen.com/indian-red-lentil-dal/>*

---

## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 Tbsp grated fresh ginger
- 1 Tbsp minced garlic
- 1 lb (2 1/4 C) red lentils
- 1 can coconut milk, plus water to make 6 C
- 3 Tbsp [garam masala](garam-masala-from-pre-ground-spices.md)
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 1.5 tsp sugar
- 2 Tbsp lemon juice

---

## Directions

Cook onions in oil over medium heat 10-15 minutes, until translucent.

Stir in ginger and garlic. Let cook 30 seconds.

Stir in lentils, coconut milk, water, garam masala, and salt.

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.

Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, until lentils reach desired texture. (We like a thick porridge, ~40 minutes.)

Stir in sugar and lemon juice.

Serve with [Naan bread](naan-bread.md).
