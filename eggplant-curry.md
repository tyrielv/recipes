# Eggplant Curry

*Adapted from "The Complete Asian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 1-2 large eggplants (we used 1 weighing 1 1/4 lb.)
* 1 14.5oz can diced tomatoes
* 3 Tbl ghee or oil
* 2 medium onions, finely chopped
* 1 1/2 tsp ginger (grated fresh or ground)
* 1/2 tsp turmeric
* 1/2 tsp chili powder
* 2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp garam masala

## Directions

Dice eggplant (no need to peel). Fry onion and ginger in ghee/oil until soft and starting to brown. Mix in spices. Stir in eggplant and tomato. Cover and simmer over low heat until vegetables are soft (20-30 minutes?), stirring occasionally.

Remove lid and continue cooking over medium heat, stirring frequently, until liquid has evaporated and curry has thickened. Serve with Indian flatbread.
