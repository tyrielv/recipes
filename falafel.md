# Falafel

*Adapted from <https://www.thekitchn.com/how-to-make-baked-falafel-in-the-oven-cooking-lessons-from-the-kitchn-219193>*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 C dried chickpeas, soaked overnight and drained
- 1 small onion, roughly chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 2 Tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp coriander
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 C flour

---

## Directions

Heat griddle over medium heat (4/10 for ours).

Combine all ingredients except baking powder and flour in food processor. Chop until thoroughly mixed, scraping the sides as necessary.

Sift in baking powder and flour. Continue mixing (either in food processor or in a bowl with a rubber spatula).

Form the falafel into patties and cook on hot griddle until browned on both sides, about 2-4 minutes per side.
