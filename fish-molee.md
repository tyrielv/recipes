# Fish Molee

*http://www.mrmoneymustache.com/2013/04/18/the-amazing-waist-slimming-wallet-fattening-nutrient/*

## Ingredients

* 1/2 lb of any white fish (tilapia, cod, swai, etc)
* 1/2 tsp turmeric
* 1 tsp salt
* 2 TBSP of coconut or olive oil
* 1 onion, diced
* 3 garlic cloves, grated
* 1 tsp of ginger, grated
* 1 TBSP of curry powder
* 1 red pepper or other big chile pepper of your choice, sliced
* 14 ounces of coconut milk

## Directions

Rub turmeric and salt onto fish.

Put oil into a big pan and start sizzling it. Dice in onion. Grate in garlic. Grate in ginger. Spoon in curry powder. Slice in red pepper. Cook for 2 more minutes.

Add about 14 ounces of coconut milk and cook for 5 more minutes, at a simmer. Add the seasoned fish and finish it up for 6-8 minutes.
