# Flour Tortillas

*Adapted from "Daily Tortilla"*

## Ingredients

* 2 3/4 C flour, plus more for rolling
* 2 tsp salt
* 2/3 C lard (shortening or oil will produce a tougher, less flaky texture)
* 3/4 C water

## Directions

Mix flour and salt. Cut in lard (James recommends using a food processor). Slowly mix in water while stirring with a fork or pastry blender - continue mixing/kneading for 1 minute. Divide into 12-16 balls (depending on desired tortilla size).

If cooking on a griddle, heat to 250 degrees. Roll out each ball into a tortilla 6-9 inches in diameter as desired, using rolling pin and flour (tortilla press tends to rip holes in this recipe, but would be a lot easier if we got the hang of it). Cook each tortilla on griddle or tortilla press for ~30-45 seconds per side, until just starting to brown.
