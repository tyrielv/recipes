# Fool-Proof Whole Wheat Bread

*Recipe from the Bosch Kitchen Center*

## Ingredients

- 6 cup warm water
- 2 tbsp salt
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2/3 cup honey
- 2 1/2 tbsp dough enhancer
- 2 tbsp gluten
- 2 1/2 tbsp instant rising yeast
- Start with 8 1/2 cup flour (total 12-15 cups)

## Directions

Combine water and 8-9 cup flour. Mix to paste consistency. Mix in yeast, then add salt, honey, oil, and gluten. Turn machine to speed 1. Add additional flour until dough pulls away from sides bowl. As motor bears down, increase to speed 2. Knead 7-8 minutes. Add dough enhancer, then knead for two more minutes. Oil hands and form loaves and place in greased pans 2/3 full. Cover with greased plastic and let raise to double. Makes about 5-6 loaves.

Bake in preheated oven at 350ºF for 30-35 minutes or until internal temperature is 180ºF.
