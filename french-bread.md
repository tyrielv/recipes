# French Bread

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 c warm water
- 2 tbsp yeast (instant)
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp salt
- about 7 c flour (I usually use white flour)

## Directions

In mixer, mix water and an equal amount of flour. Add yeast and sugar, mix slightly. Add oil, salt, and another cup of flour; mix. While machine is mixing on low, add flour until dough pulls away from sides of bowl. Cover and mix on speed 2 for 6 minutes. Turn out on an oiled counter. Form into rolls or 2 loaves\*. If doing french bread, after making loaves and placing in pan, use a sharp knife to cut a few diagonal slits in the top of the loaf. Beat egg whites, and brush egg whites on tops of the loaves. Allow bread to raise until doubled in size, then bake at 400ºF (don't preheat oven) for 15 minutes, until golden brown, or until internal bread temperature is 180ºF.

\*To form into a french bread loaf, use your knuckles to pound out a dough round into a elongated oval (the dough should be about 3/4" thick). Starting with a long side, roll the dough. Fold the ends over to form a nice long roll of dough.
