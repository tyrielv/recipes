# Fried Fish Taquitos

*Adapted from "Daily Tortilla"*

## Ingredients

* 2 Tbl olive oil
* 1/2 medium onion, diced
* 1 Tbl dried cilantro
* 1 tsp cumin
* 1 jalapeño or serrano, chopped (or cayenne pepper to taste)
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 1/4 lb. (5 filets) tilapia
* 1/2 C enchilada sauce
* Oil
* ~12 corn tortillas

## Directions

Saute onion and spices in olive oil for 2 min. Add fish and continue to cook until fish is flaky, about 5 minutes. Stir in enchilada sauce and remove from heat.

Fry tortillas in oil on medium-high heat for 6 seconds per side. Add ~2 Tbl fish filling, roll, and pin in place with tooth picks. Either fry for 2 minutes, or bake for 10-20 minutes at 425, until crispy.
