# Greek Chicken and Rice Soup with Egg and Lemon (Avgolemono)

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/9476-greek-chicken-and-rice-soup-with-egg-and-lemon-avgolemono)*

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs (2-3) chicken breasts
- 1 1/4 tsp salt
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 1 Tbsp lemon extract
- 3 tsp dill
- 1 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- 3/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 clove minced garlic
- 1 cup uncooked white rice, preferably long grain
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 4.5 Tbsp lemon juice

## Directions

Filet chicken breasts to no more than 1/2" thick. Sprinkle on both sides with 1 3/4 tsp salt and let stand at room temperature 15-30 minutes.

Bring 6 cups chicken broth, 1 tbsp lemon extract, 3 tsp dill, 1 1/2 tsp coriander, 3/4 tsp black pepper, 1 clove garlic, and 1 cup uncooked rice to a boil on high heat in a stockpot, stirring occasionally. Cover and cook on low heat 5 min.

Add chicken. Turn off heat and let sit, covered, for 15 minutes. (Do not stir or uncover).

Remove chicken, cube, and set aside.

Using a slotted spoon, remove 1 cup of cooked rice. Blend the 1 cup of cooked rice with 2 eggs, 1 egg yolk, and 4 1/2 tbsp lemon juice. Set aside.

Return chicken to pot and bring to simmer (195º after stirring) over high heat. Remove from heat and stir in egg mixture.
