# Ground Beef Enchiladas

*Adapted from [Cook's Illustrated](https://www.cooksillustrated.com/recipes/10313-ground-beef-and-cheese-enchiladas)*

Preheat oven to 400ºF

## Ingredients

### Sauce

- 2 C water
- 4 dried chiles (ancho or similar), stemmed and seeded
- 2 tsp beef bouillon
- 1 tsp liquid smoke
- 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 Tbl vegetable oil
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 6 cloves minced garlic
- 1/4 C tomato paste
- 1 tsp cumin

### Enchiladas

- 1 lb. lean ground beef
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp cilantro
- 1/2 tsp oregano
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 8 oz finely shredded cheese, divided - jack, cheddar, or similar.
- 12 corn tortillas
- 2 Tbl vegetable oil

## Directions

### Sauce

Bring 2 C water to a boil. Rip the chilies into small pieces and add chilies, bouillon, liquid smoke, and apple cider vinegar to the hot water. Allow to sit while the onions cook.

Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high. Sauté onions 5-10 minutes until translucent. Add garlic, tomato paste, and cumin. Cook 5 more minutes, stirring frequently. Add to the chili mixture and puree until smooth. Salt to taste.

### Enchiladas

Heat skillet over medium. Add ground beef and seasonings. Cook until no longer pink. Remove from heat and stir in 6 oz. of the cheese and 1/4 C enchilada sauce. Salt to taste.

Spread vegetable oil on both sides of corn tortillas, and spread out on a 9x13 baking dish. Bake for 5 minutes to soften. Remove from pan and spread 1/2 C enchilada sauce over the bottom of the pan.

Fill tortillas with scant 1/4 C of beef filling, roll tightly and place in the baking dish in two rows. Spread remaining sauce over enchiladas, sprinkle with remaining cheese, and bake for ~15 minutes until cheese is lightly browned and sauce is bubbling at the edges.
