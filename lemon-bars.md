# Lemon Bars

*Adapted from <https://www.thepioneerwoman.com/food-cooking/recipes/a12104/lemon-bars/>*

Double recipe for a 9x13 pan.

---

## Crust

### Ingredients

- 1 c flour
- 1/4 c sugar
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1 stick (1/2 c) room temperature, salted butter, cut into small cubes

### Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 350˚F.
2. Grease a 9x9 pan. OP (original poster) suggested butter. I used non-stick spray, which worked well enough.
3. Stir together the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the butter to the bowl and use a pastry cutter to cut it all together until the mixture resembles fine crumbs. Press into the prepared pan and bake until golden around the edges, about 20 minutes.

---

## Filling

### Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c sugar
- 1/4 c flour
- 4 whole large eggs
- 1/2 c lemon juice (OP used juice from 4 medium-sized lemons)
- 2 tbsp lemon extract (OP used zest from the 4 lemons)
- powdered sugar, for sifting

### Directions

1. Stir together the flour and sugar.
2. Crack in the eggs and whisk to combine.
3. Add the lemon juice and lemon extract.
4. Pour over crust and bake about 20 minutes, or until the filling is set.
5. Allow to cool in fridge a minimum of 2 hours. (We sped up the process by allowing it to chill for 30-40 minutes in the freezer.)
6. Once chilled, sift powdered sugar over the top.
