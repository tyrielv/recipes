# Minced Meat and Split Pea Curry

*Adapted from "The Complete Asian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 1/2 C split peas
* 2 Tbl ghee or oil
* 2 medium onions, finely chopped
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 tsp ginger (grated fresh or dried)
* 1 tsp ground turmeric
* 1/2 tsp chili powder
* 1 tsp dried cilantro
* 1 14.5oz can diced tomatoes
* 2 tsp salt
* 1 lb. ground beef, turkey or pork
* 2 tsp garam masala

## Directions

Wash split peas and soak while preparing other ingredients.

Fry onions, garlic, and ginger in ghee/oil until soft. Add turmeric, chili powder, cilantro, tomatoes, and salt. Cook on medium heat for 5 minutes. Add meat and drained split peas. Stir until mixed, then cover and cook for 40 minutes or until peas are tender, stirring occasionally. Add water if too much evaporates.

Add garam masala and cook, stirring frequently, until liquid has evaporated and it starts to fry (this is a dry curry). Serve with Indian flatbread.
