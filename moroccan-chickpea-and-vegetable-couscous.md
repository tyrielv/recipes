# Moroccan Chickpea and Vegetable Couscous

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/5406-moroccan-chickpea-and-vegetable-couscous)*

## Ingredients

- 4 Tbl butter, divided
- 2 C plain couscous
- 1 onion, minced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 1 tsp salt (plus more to taste)
- 4 cloves minced garlic
- 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp ground ginger
- 2 C chicken broth (add an additional 1/2 tsp salt if using homemade stock)
- 2 C water
- 1 can chickpeas or 1 C dry chickpeas, cooked
- 1 1/2 C frozen peas or shelled edamame

## Directions

Heat 2 Tbl butter in skillet over medium-high heat. After foam subsides, stir in 2 cups plain couscous. Cook 3+ minutes, stirring occasionally, to toast it. Remove couscous and set aside.

Heat remaining 2 Tbl butter over medium heat. Add 1 minced onion, 2 diced carrots, and 1 tsp salt. Cook for 5-10 minutes until onions are soft and turning brown. Stir in 4 cloves of minced garlic, 1/2 tsp coriander and 1/2 tsp ginger.

Add 2 cups chicken broth, 2 cups water, and (1 cup dry, cooked) chickpeas, and bring to a boil. Stir in couscous and 1 1/2 cups of peas or edamame. Remove from heat, cover, and let sit for 12 minutes. Stir to fluff and salt to taste.

## Notes

Goes well with chicken. Serves 6-8.
