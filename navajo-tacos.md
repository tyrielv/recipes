# Navajo Tacos

My mom got this recipe from a Navajo lady that lived in one of the wards I grew up in. Love it!

## Ingredients

**Scones (makes 20):**

Sift together:
- 10 c flour
- 2 tsp salt
- 10 heaping tsp baking powder

Stir in:
- 1 qt + 2-3 tbsp tepid water

**Topping Ideas:**
- Chili
- Sour cream
- Guacamole or avocado
- Chopped tomatoes (or salsa)
- Grated cheese
- Sliced olives
- Onion
- Shredded Lettuce

## Directions

Divide dough into 20 portions and allow to rest covered for at least 20 minutes.

Deep fry until golden brown.
