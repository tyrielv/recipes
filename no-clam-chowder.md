# No-Clam Chowder

*Adapted from [Food Network](http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/dave-lieberman/new-england-clam-chowder-recipe.html) and [Food Network](http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/anne-burrell/new-england-clam-chowder-recipe.html)*

## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp butter
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 stalks celery, diced
- 1 lb potatoes, diced
- 1/2 C water
- 1/2 tsp shrimp paste
- 1 Tbsp fish sauce
- 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 bay leaves
- 3 Tbsp flour
- 1 1/2 C whole milk
- 1 1/2 C heavy cream
- 1 lb tilapia (~5 fillets)

## Directions

Sauté 1 large diced onion and 4 stalks of diced celery in 2 tbsp butter until soft, 5-10 minutes. Add 1 lb diced potatoes, 1/2 cup water, 1/2 tsp shrimp paste, 1 tbsp fish sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, 1/2 tsp salt, and 2 bay leaves. Bring to a simmer, then simmer over medium-low heat for 20 minutes. Remove the bay leaves. Optionally purée the vegetables. Stir in 3 tbsp flour until dissolved, then add 1 1/2 cups milk and 1 1/2 cups cream. Bring back to a simmer, stirring frequently. Add 1 lb tilapia and cook until it flakes apart when stirring, about 10-15 minutes.
