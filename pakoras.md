# Pakoras

*Adapted from "The Complete Asian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 2 cups Chickpea flour ("Besan")
  * If grinding dry chickpeas, about 1 1/3 cups
* 1 C water
* 1 tsp garam masala
* 1/2 tsp turmeric
* 1/2 tsp chili powder
* 1-2 tsp salt, to taste
* 4 cups minced vegetables (such as bell pepper, carrot, onion, potato, cauliflower, celery, eggplant, etc.)

## Directions

Preheat oven to 500º. Whisk water into flour to make a thick batter. Mix in spices and vegetables. Place spoonfuls of mixture on a greased cookie sheet. Bake for ~18 minutes.

(Original recipe called for deep-frying in small batches until golden brown instead of baking.)
