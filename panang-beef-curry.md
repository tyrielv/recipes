# Panang Beef Curry

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/9628-panang-beef-curry)*

## Ingredients

- 2 lb beef, such as chuck or round steak, cut into strips ~2 in. square by 1/4" thick.
- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 2-4 Tbsp red curry paste
- 1 14 oz can coconut milk
- 4 tsp fish sauce
- 2 tsp sugar
- Zest of one lime, or 1 Tbsp lime juice
- Zest of one lemon, or 1 tsp lemon extract or 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1/3 C chopped nuts (peanuts or cashews)

## Directions

Simmer beef in just enough water to cover for 1-2 hours until tender (time varies depending on the cut of beef). Drain and set aside. (This can be done up to a day before).

Heat vegetable oil in non-stick skillet. Stir in 2 Tbsp red curry paste and sauté, stirring constantly, for 5-10 minutes (until darkened). Stir in coconut milk, fish sauce, and sugar. Taste for spiciness - if not enough, add up to 2 Tbl curry paste. Stir in beef and bring to a rapid simmer.

Simmer uncovered for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sauce is thickened. Stir in lime and lemon and cook additional 2 minutes. Serve over rice, with nuts sprinkled over the top.
