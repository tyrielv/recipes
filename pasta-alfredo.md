# Pasta Alfredo

*From "The Classic Italian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 1 C heavy cream, divided (1 1/4c, divided)
* 3 Tbl butter (4 tbsp)
* Salt to taste
* 12 oz. dry pasta (fettuccine and rotini both work well) (16oz)
* 2/3 C (2.35oz) grated parmesan (the real stuff melts smoother than the pre-grated Kraft stuff, but we can't tell the taste apart to be honest) (1 cup or about 3.5 oz)
* 6 twists of pepper from a pepper mill. (7)
* pinch of nutmeg

## Directions

In a large pan (eventually it will hold everything) put in 2/3 C cream and all the butter. Bring to a simmer just until thickened (should be about 1 minute once it reaches simmer).

Meanwhile bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. (4 qts, 2 Tbl salt). Boil the pasta for a minute or two less than package directions. It should still be very firm. Drain thoroughly.

Turn heat to low under sauce pan. Add pasta to sauce pan and toss to coat. Add the rest of the cream, cheese, pepper, nutmeg, and salt to taste (1/2 tsp to start). Continue tossing until thoroughly coated, and cream has thickened.
