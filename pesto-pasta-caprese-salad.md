# Pesto Pasta Caprese Salad


## Ingredients

- 12 oz farfalle (bowtie) or similar pasta
- Olive oil, to keep pasta from sticking together after it cooks
- 3/4 cup whole olives, drained
- 3/4 cup grape or cherry tomatoes, rinsed
- 3/4 cup summer sausage or similar meat (my kids love pepperoni instead)
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella (I like more)

### For the sauce

- 1 tbsp sun dried tomatoes
- 1/4 cup pesto (we used Costco's pesto sauce)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

## Directions

Cook and drain pasta according to package directions. Drizzle with olive oil to keep from sticking. Allow to cool slightly.

While the pasta is cooking, rinse and drain tomatoes and olives, then cut in half. Cube the sausage and mozzarella. Add to pasta after pasta has cooled slightly.

For the sauce, put sun dried tomatoes, pesto, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar into a food processor (I used an immersion blender) and blend until smooth. Add to pasta salad and toss well. Chill before serving.

## Notes

I saw a recipe that called for "sun-dried tomato pesto sauce", which I didn't have but sounded good, so this was a simplified version of a [recipe](http://allrecipes.com/recipe/25307/sun-dried-tomato-pesto/) I found on that. I actually have a hard time with vinegar, so I only put half of the sauce on the entire dish, and left the remaining half out in a little bowl for anyone who wanted more sauce.
