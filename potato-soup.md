# Potato Soup

## Ingredients

- Oil for sautéing
- 3 leeks, chopped
- 3 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
- 1 1/2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp chopped onion or 2 tsp dried, minced onion
- 4 c chicken broth

**To add later (optional):**
- 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
- 2-3 carrots, chopped
- Head of broccoli, chopped
- Two 1/4" slices of deli ham, diced*
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

## Directions

In a stockpot, heat oil and then add leeks, potatoes, garlic, and onion. Sauté for 5-10 minutes, or until tender. Add chicken broth, bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes or until potatoes are cooked through. Blend the soup. Add whatever else you want to the soup and simmer until added veggies are cooked through.

## Notes

*If you choose to add ham, wait until the last minute or so of simmering and just heat the ham through. Otherwise the whole soup will taste like ham.

This soup goes well with [bread sticks](chicken-parmesan-and-bread-sticks.md).
