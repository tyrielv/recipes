# Asian-style Sweet & Sour Sauce

*Adapted from <https://avirtualvegan.com/sticky-chili-ginger-tofu/>*

---

## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp corn starch
- 1/4 Cup vinegar
- 1 Cup water
- 1/2 Cup ketchup
- 1/4 Cup honey
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp sesame oil
- 1/2 tsp 5-spice powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1/4 tsp chili flakes
- 2 tsp minced garlic
- 1 Tbsp grated fresh ginger (or 1 tsp dry)

---

## Directions

Mix corn starch with vinegar in a small sauce pan until thoroughly blended.

Mix in other ingredients.

Cook on medium, stirring frequently, until sauce is thickened and bubbling, about 10 minutes.

Serve over tofu, chicken, etc.

Freezes well.

Makes twice as much as we typically use in a meal.
