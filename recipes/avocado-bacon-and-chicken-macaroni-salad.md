# Avocado, Bacon, and Chicken Macaroni Salad

*Adapted from <https://damndelicious.net/2013/06/28/bacon-and-avocado-macaroni-salad/>*

---

## Ingredients

**For the salad:**

- 12 oz macaroni-type pasta* (or other, like farfalle)
- 1-2 cups chicken, cooked and cubed (I sometimes use meat from a rotisserie chicken)
- 5-6 slices of bacon, fried and diced**
- 2 ripe avocados*, diced
- Salt and pepper to taste

**For the dressing*:**

- 3/4 cup mayo
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 1/2 tbsp lemon zest (I subbed in 1/2 tsp lemon extract when I forgot to buy a lemon)
- 1 tsp thyme
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1/3 cup olive oil.

---

## Directions

Boil pasta until cooked according to package directions. Drain. Meanwhile, combine all ingredients for dressing and mix well and set aside. In a large bowl, combine pasta, bacon, avocado, and chicken. Mix in dressing.

---

## Notes

*If you are not serving immediately, I would recommend stirring a little olive oil into the pasta to keep it from sticking immediately after draining, and then wait to dice the avocados and hold off putting the dressing on the salad until right before serving for freshness purposes.

**I have found it easier to microwave the bacon while I'm preparing the rest of the food.
