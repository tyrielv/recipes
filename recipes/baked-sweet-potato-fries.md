# Baked Sweet Potato Fries*

*From http://www.ourbestbites.com/2009/03/baked-sweet-potato-fries-with-honey-lime-dip/*

This one is really easy. Cut a sweet potato or yam into french-fry-size pieces, dredge in olive oil, and bake for 10-15 minutes per side at 425ºF. You can sprinkle with spices/salt before baking, but it really isn't needed.

## Notes

*Here is the original recipe in case you'd like to check it out. It also included a Honey-Lime dip recipe, but we didn't actually try that. Have fun!
