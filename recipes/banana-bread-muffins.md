# Banana Bread / Muffins

*Adapted from "Joy of Cooking"*

## Ingredients

- 1/3 C shortening
- 2/3 C sugar
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp lemon extract (optional)
- 2 eggs
- ~1 C mashed ripe banana (about 2-3 bananas)
- 1 3/4 C all-purpose flour
- 2 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 C chopped nuts (optional)
- 1/4 C chopped dried fruits (optional)

## Directions

Preheat oven to 350º

Cream shortening and sugar. Beat in extracts, eggs, and banana. Whisk dry ingredients together. Beat dry ingredients into the batter 1/3 at a time.

Muffins (about 12) take about 20 minutes, small loaves (about 3-4) take about 40 minutes, and a large loaf takes about 60 minutes.
