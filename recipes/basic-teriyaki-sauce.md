# Basic Teriyaki Sauce

I can't remember why we haven't been buying Teriyaki sauce recently, but this all started a few months ago when we were trying to save money buying foods. We found a bottle of some sort of "Teriyaki" inspired sauce at Costco, but it turned out to be nasty. Later, I think we were making dinner for friends, but one of them couldn't have one of the ingredients found in most Teriyaki sauces and we ended up making our own. Here is a very basic recipe that works well for most of our recipes that use it:

## Ingredients

- 1/4 c water
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- about 1 tsp sugar (to taste)

## Directions

Mix all ingredients together.
