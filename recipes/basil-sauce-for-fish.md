# Basil Sauce for Fish

## Ingredients

- 1 Tbl dry basil (or 4 Tbl chopped fresh basil)
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 Tbl parmesan cheese (.35 oz)
- 4 tsp water
- 1/4 tsp chicken bouillon
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp minced garlic

## Directions

Mix together in a small bowl. Spoon over baked fish (it goes well with cod).

If you use dry basil, I would recommend putting the sauce together before baking the fish so that the basil has some time to soak and rehydrate.
