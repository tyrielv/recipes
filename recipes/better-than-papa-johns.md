# Pizza (and Pizza Sauce)

We have decided that this is our favorite pizza dough recipe we've made thus far. It's supposed to be very similar to the recipe used at Papa John's. We tried it, and liked it! When I asked how it compared to his favorite pizza (Papa John's) he said, "It's as good, if not better than theirs!"

## Ingredients

**Pizza dough:**
- 2 1/2 - 3 c flour
- 1/2 tsp white sugar
- 2 1/4 tsp instant yeast
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 c warm water
- 2 tbsp oil

## Directions

**Preparation:**

Combine 1 cup flour, sugar, yeast, and salt, blending well. Add oil and water gradually, until well moistened, and then stir until a smooth batter is created. Add additional flour by hand until the dough pulls away from the sides of the bowl.

Turn out onto a floured surface and knead in more flour until a smooth, elastic dough is created. Cover loosely with plastic and allow to rest about 15 minutes in a warm place.

Preheat oven to 450ºF.

Press dough into 2 twelve inch pizza pans. (An alternative option is to roll out onto parchment paper and bake on a stone in the oven for a crispier crust.) Allow to rest for 10-15 minutes in a warm place. Brush crusts with oil and pre-bake for 5 minutes.

Top as you wish, then bake again until crust is crisp and cheese is melted. Enjoy!

## Notes

For pizza sauce, see [Pizza Sauce](pizza-sauce.md) — a newer recipe we like much better!

**Pizza Sauce** (old recipe):

(This is a combination of many recipes I looked at on the internet, and it turned out delicious!)

- 1 (8oz) can tomato sauce
- 1/4 c water
- 3/4 tsp dried oregano
- 3/4 tsp dried basil
- 3/4 tsp minced garlic
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- couple of pinches of salt
- dash of lemon juice
- dash of olive oil

Mix all ingredients together in a small sauce pan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
