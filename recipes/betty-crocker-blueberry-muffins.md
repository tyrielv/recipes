# Betty Crocker Blueberry Muffins

*Source: <https://www.food.com/recipe/betty-crocker-blueberry-muffins-252834>*

**Preheat oven:** 400°F
**Yield:** 12 muffins

---

## Ingredients

- 3 cups sifted flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups blueberries (I used frozen)

---

## Instructions

- Preheat oven to 400 F.
- Sift together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
- In a different bowl beat together shortening, egg, and milk.
- Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients. Will be quite thick.
- If using frozen blueberries, rinse them until the water runs clear, then pat them dry with a paper towel. Carefully fold blueberries into the batter. Don't worry if you crush a couple, the juices make a nice color swirl.
- Divide batter evenly between 12 medium muffin tins, greased. (I did not use paper muffin cups.)
- Bake at 400 F for 20-25 minutes until lightly golden and an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
