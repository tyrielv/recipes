# Betty Crocker Homemade Fudge Brownies

*Source: <https://www.food.com/recipe/betty-crocker-fudge-brownies-homemade-496992>*

---

Preheat oven to 350°F.

Sift together with a whisk:

- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/3 c cocoa
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp baking powder

Add in:

- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup vegetable or canola oil
- 1 tsp vanilla

Gently whisk wet ingredients into dry until just blended. DO NOT OVERMIX.

Pour into greased 9x9 pan. Bake at 350°F for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
