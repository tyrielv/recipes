# Biscuits

*Adapted from "100% Natural Lard: The Lost Art of Cooking with Your Grandmother's Secret Ingredient"*

**Notes for next time: Try 1/2 C lard and 3/4 tsp salt**

## Ingredients

* 1/3 C lard
* 2 1/2 C flour
* 1 Tbl baking powder
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 C milk

## Directions

Preheat oven to 400ºF.

Mix 2 C flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Cut in lard. Add milk and stir.

Sprinkle remaining 1/2 C of flour on a clean, flat surface. Dump dough out on it, and knead for 5 minutes (most of the 1/2 C flour should get worked in).

Roll out until 1 inch thick, and cut into 12 pieces (use a cookie cutter if you want pretty round biscuits). Bake 15-20 minutes on greased cookie sheet, until tops begin to brown.
