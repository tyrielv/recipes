# Braised Carrots

*Adapted from "Mastering the Art of French Cooking"*

## Ingredients

* 1 lb carrots, cut into bite-size pieces
* 2 tsp sugar
* 1 C water
* 1 Tb butter
* 1/4 tsp salt, more to taste
* pepper to taste

## Directions

Bring all ingredients to a boil. Boil slowly for 30-40 minutes until carrots are tender and liquid is (mostly) gone.

Original recipe says to boil covered, but the liquid never seems to go away if it's covered.

If cooking a larger batch, use less water or split into multiple pans or the liquid won't boil off.
