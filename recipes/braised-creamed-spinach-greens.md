# Braised/Creamed Spinach/Greens

*Adapted from "Mastering the Art of French Cooking"*

## Ingredients

* 3 lbs fresh spinach OR 1-2 bunches other greens OR 26 oz. frozen spinach [30-32 oz. frozen spinach measurements in brackets]
* 3 1/2 Tbl butter, divided [4 Tbl]
* 1 1/2 Tbl flour [5 tsp]
* 1 C beef broth OR heavy cream [10 fl. oz]
* salt and pepper

## Directions

If using frozen spinach, thaw it beforehand and skip the next paragraph.

If using fresh greens, cut out the stems, chop them up (alternatively, you can chop after blanching), and rinse very well. Bring 8 quarts of water (with 4 Tbl salt) up to a heavy boil. Blanch the greens by adding them to the boiling water for about 5 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water in colander. Squeeze as much water out as possible.

Heat 2 Tbl butter [2 Tbl 1 tsp] in a saucepan over medium-high. When bubbling, stir in the greens. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the moisture from the spinach has cooked off. Sprinkle with flour, stir, and cook 2 more minutes. Reduce heat. Stir in broth or cream. Cover and simmer on low for 15 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent sticking. Add water by teaspoons if it becomes too dry.

Stir in remaining butter. Add salt and pepper to taste.
