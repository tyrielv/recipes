# Braised Onions

*Adapted from "Mastering the Art of French Cooking"*

## Ingredients

* 18-24 pearl onions, peeled, or 1-2 large onions coarsely chopped
* 1 1/2 Tb butter
* 1 1/2 Tb oil
* 1/2 C beef bouillon (or water)
* salt and pepper to taste
* 2 tsp dried parsley
* 1/2 bay leaf
* 1/4 tsp. thyme

## Directions

Heat butter and oil in skillet until it starts bubbling. Add onions and sauté for ~10 minutes. Add liquid and spices. Simmer slowly for 40-50 minutes until tender (but not mush) and liquid has evaporated. (Original recipe says to cover, but liquid may not evaporate if covered.)
