# Carrot-Ginger Soup

*Adapted from [The America's Test Kitchen Quick Family Cookbook](https://www.amazon.com/Americas-Kitchen-Quick-Family-Cookbook/dp/1933615990/)*

## Ingredients

- 1 large onion, minced
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 2 Tbsp grated fresh ginger
- 1 1/2 lb carrots, diced
- 3 C chicken broth
- 3/4 C whole milk
- Juice of 1 orange (~1/4 C)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

Saute onions in butter until soft (~5 minutes). Add ginger and stir for 30 seconds. Add carrots and broth; bring to a boil, then simmer covered on low for 15+ minutes until carrots are tender. Purée, then stir in milk and orange juice. Season to taste (1 tsp salt if using homemade broth).
