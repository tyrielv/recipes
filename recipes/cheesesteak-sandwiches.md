# Cheesesteak Sandwiches

*From "Our Best Bites: Mormon Moms in the Kitchen"; pg 127.*

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 lb boneless steak, sliced very thinly
- 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
- salt
- ground pepper
- hot sauce
- sliced provolone
- 4-6 hoagie rolls

## Directions

Melt butter in large skillet. Add steak and sauté until halfway cooked, then add onion and green peppers. Season with salt, pepper, and hot sauce. Continue cooking until meat is cooked through and onion and peppers are tender. Serve on hoagie rolls with provolone cheese.
