# Cheesy Polenta with Zucchini and Tomatoes

*Adapted from [The America's Test Kitchen Quick Family Cookbook](https://www.amazon.com/Americas-Kitchen-Quick-Family-Cookbook/dp/1933615990/)*

## Ingredients

- 1 C polenta
- 4 C boiling water
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1.5 oz grated parmesan
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, 1/2 inch cubes
- 1 lb zucchini, chopped
- 2 Tbl Olive oil
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 14 oz can diced tomatoes
- 1/2 C pesto

## Directions

**For the polenta:** Stir boiling water and salt into polenta for 5 minutes if using instant polenta, or for 25-30 minutes over a double boiler if using regular polenta. Remove from heat, stir in parmesan, and add salt and pepper to taste.

**For the vegetables:** Put mozzarella cubes on a plate in the freezer for 10-15 minutes. Meanwhile, sauté zucchini, olive oil, and salt 5 minutes. Stir in tomatoes and continue cooking 5-10 minutes. Remove from heat, stir in pesto, then stir in chilled mozzarella.

Serve the zucchini mixture over polenta.
