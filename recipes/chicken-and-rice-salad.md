# Chicken and Rice Salad

## Ingredients

- 3 cups cooked rice
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts; cooked and cubed
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 cup cashews
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 cup chopped celery
- 6 green onions, chopped
- Salt and Pepper to taste

## Directions

Combine all ingredients in bowl and mix well. Serve cold.
