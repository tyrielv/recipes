# Chicken Tikka Masala

*Adapted from http://thepioneerwoman.com/cooking/2009/06/chicken-tikka-masala-by-pastor-ryan/*

## Ingredients

**Rice:**
- 2 C Basmati rice
- 4 Tbl Butter
- 1 tsp Salt
- 1 Tbl Turmeric
- Optional - frozen peas

**Chicken Tikka Masala:**
- 2 Chicken breasts, cut up
  - or 1 chicken breast and 1 can (1 C dry) chickpeas, cooked and drained
- 4 Tbl Butter
- 1 Onion, chopped
- 4 Cloves Garlic or 1 Tbl minced garlic
- 2 inches fresh ginger piece, grated, or 1 Tbl ground ginger
- 3 Tbl [Garam Masala](garam-masala-from-pre-ground-spices.md)
- 1 Tbl Salt
- 1 Tbl Sugar
- Optional - 1 Tbl dried cilantro
- Optional - jalapeno pepper (chopped) or cayenne to taste (1/8 tsp)
- 1 large can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
- 2 C Heavy cream or 1 can coconut milk

## Directions

Cook rice and seasonings in rice cooker. Stir in frozen peas when done.

Sauté chicken in butter until cooked through. Add onion and cook ~5 minutes. Add seasonings and cook another 1-2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes and turn heat down to medium. Simmer for at least 5 minutes, longer if you have time. Stir in heavy cream or coconut milk.
