# Chinese 5-Spice Beef

*Adapted from "The Complete Asian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 1 lb beef, sliced very thinly
* 1 clove minced garlic
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp ginger
* 1 Tbl soy sauce
* 1/2 tsp 5-spice powder
* 2 tsp corn starch
* 2 Tbl oil
* 3 Tbl water

## Directions

Mix garlic, salt, ginger, soy, 5-spice powder, and corn starch into beef.

Heat oil in wok over high. Add beef when hot. Stir-fry until no longer pink. Add water and continue stirring until thickened. Serve with rice and stir-fry vegetables.
