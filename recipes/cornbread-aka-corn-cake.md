# Cornbread (aka Corn Cake)

---

## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 1/3 Cups sugar (also works well with 1 cup)
- 1 Cup cornmeal
- 2 TBSP baking powder
- 1 TSP salt
- 2 1/2 cups milk
- 4 eggs
- 2/3 cup oil (you can substitute apple sauce)
- 6 TBSP melted butter

---

## Directions

Mix well, bake at 350 degrees until inserted toothpick comes out clean: about 35 minutes for a 9x13 pan, about 17 minutes for muffins.
