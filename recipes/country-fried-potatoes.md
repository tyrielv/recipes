# Country Fried Potatoes

---

## Ingredients (for one person)

- 1 large potato, peeled and diced into bite-sized pieces
- 1-2 tbsp oil, for pan frying
- salt and pepper, to taste

---

## Directions

Boil the potatoes (I don't have a set amount--I base it on how many people will be eating and how big) until cooked through, about 10 minutes. Drain.

While potatoes are draining, put the oil in a frying pan and bring up to heat on med to med-high. Add potatoes and sprinkle with salt/pepper to taste. Fry without stirring until potatoes are golden-brown on one side. Turn over and brown on the other side.

Total cooking time about 40 minutes. If I want these for breakfast, I like to boil the potatoes the night before (then store in the fridge) to reduce the amount of cooking time in the morning.
