# Cream Sauce for Chicken or Turkey

*Adapted from "Mastering the Art of French Cooking"*

## Ingredients

* Drippings or pan scrapings from cooking chicken or turkey, water added to make 1/2 C, if you have them; 7 Tbl water, 1 Tbl butter otherwise.
* 1/2 tsp chicken bouillon
* 1 Tbl white wine vinegar
* 1 Tbl parsley
* 1 Tbl flour
* 1 C cream
* Salt and pepper to taste
* Lemon juice to taste

## Directions

Bring drippings/scrapings/water/butter to a boil. Add bouillon, parsley and vinegar and boil for 1 minute. Sprinkle in flour and stir until smooth. Stir in cream and boil until thickened. Remove from heat and season with salt, pepper, and drops of lemon juice to taste.
