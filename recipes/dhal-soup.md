# Dhal Soup

*Adapted from http://www.realsustainablehabits.com/2012/07/27/sweet-dhal-meatless-monday-series/*

## Ingredients

- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 Tbl butter
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 can coconut milk
- 15oz can diced tomatoes
- 3 cups water
- 1 1/2 C lentils
- 2+ tsp salt (to taste)

## Directions

Saute onion, butter, and garlic for 3 minutes. Add spices except for salt and cook for 2 minutes. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until lentils are tender (~25-45 minutes).
