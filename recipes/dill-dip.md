# Dill Dip

This is the ONLY way I really ever eat plain potato chips. :) Also, the fresher the dill weed the better!

## Ingredients

- 1/4 c sour cream
- 1/4 c mayonnaise
- 3/4 tsp dill weed
- 3/4 tsp Bon Appétit Seasoning Salt
- 3/4 tsp dry minced onion

## Directions

Mix all ingredients together in a small bowl. Serve with chips or veggies!
