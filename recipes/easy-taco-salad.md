# Easy Taco Salad

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed (I don't care for kidney beans, so I use black beans instead)
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 onion, chopped
- 1 medium tomato, diced (optional)
- 1 head lettuce, shredded
- shredded cheese of your choice
- Italian dressing to taste
- Hot sauce to taste
- 1 can refried beans (optional)
- Plain Doritos or Tortilla chips

## Directions

Brown ground beef (and drain fat as browning), then add beans and salt. Heat until warmed through, then add onion and toss well. Add meat/bean mixture to a large bowl with shredded lettuce. Toss with shredded cheese, dressing, and hot sauce to taste.

Serve over hot refried beans (optional) and chips. (Tip: unless you like soggy chips, wait to add chips until you want to eat the salad. We usually let everyone add chips to their own salad instead of adding chips to the mixing bowl and letting the chips sit and soak up moisture.) Salad can also be served with salsa/tomatoes, sour cream, and guacamole.
