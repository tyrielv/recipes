# Fajita Chicken


## Ingredients

- 1/4 Cup vegetable oil + 1 Tbsp
- 2 Tbsp lime juice
- 4 cloves minced garlic
- 1 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1.5 lbs chicken breast (2 or 3), filleted or pounded flat to 1/2" thickness

## Directions

Mix 1/4 C oil and seasonings in a large bowl.

Add chicken and toss to coat.

Allow to sit at room temperature for 30-60 minutes.

Preheat oven to 225º and position rack at lowest point.

Heat 1 Tbsp oil in a nonstick skillet on high just until smoking.

Add chicken, shaking off excess marinade, and let cook without moving, for 5 minutes.

Flip chicken, then transfer skillet to lowest rack in oven.

Bake until chicken reaches 160º internally, about 7-15 minutes.

Let chicken rest on cutting board for 5 minutes, then slice. Toss with pan juices.
