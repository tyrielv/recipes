# Fluffy Pancakes

*Adapted from [Cafe Delites](https://cafedelites.com/best-fluffy-pancakes/)*

## Ingredients

- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 8 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 1/4 cups milk
- 1/2 cup melted butter (I used oil because I'm too lazy to melt butter)
- 4 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs

## Directions

Sift dry ingredients together. Whisk in wet ingredients. Cook on a pre-heated griddle on a medium-low temperature until golden brown on each side.

Makes about 24 pancakes.
