# French Artisan Bread with Flavored Oils for Dipping

## Ingredients

**Bread:**
- 3-3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup warm water
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 to 2 tsp salt
- 2 1/4 tsp instant yeast
- 1 cup sesame seeds
- Additional 1/2 cup seeds

## Directions

In a mixing bowl, mix 1 cup water with 1 cup of flour until paste consistency. Add yeast and mix. Add sugar and salt and mix well. Begin adding remaining flour gradually. Once flour is completely incorporated, add cup of sesame seeds and knead into bread dough. Continue kneading for 5-10 minutes or until bread dough is supple and non-sticky. Form into ball and roll the top and sides of ball in the rest of the sesame seeds. Place the dough on parchment paper or on lightly sprayed baking sheet. Cover slightly with damp dish towel or lightly sprayed plastic wrap and let rise until doubled in size.

Bake in preheated oven at 375ºF for 30 minutes (or less, depending upon how dark you like your crust).

## Herb and Spice Blend Oil

- 1 tsp. crushed red pepper
- 1 tsp. ground black pepper
- 1 tsp. dried oregano
- 1 tsp. dried rosemary
- 1 tsp. dried basil
- 1 tsp. parsley flakes
- 1 tsp. granulated garlic
- 1 tsp. minced garlic
- 1 tsp. kosher salt
- 1/4 - 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Combine all ingredients, except oil, on a shallow bowl or plate. Pour olive oil over.

---

## Parmesan Pepper Oil

- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Combine all ingredients, except oil, on a shallow bowl or plate. Pour olive oil over.
