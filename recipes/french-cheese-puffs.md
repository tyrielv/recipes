# French Cheese Puffs

*Adapted from [Curious Cuisiniere](https://www.curiouscuisiniere.com/gougeres-french-cheese-puffs/)*

## Ingredients

- 1 C water
- 1/2 C (1 stick) butter
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 C flour
- 4 eggs
- 2 oz parmesan or similar cheese, grated

## Directions

Preheat oven to 450ºF

Heat water, butter, and salt in a sauce pan (preferably non-stick) over medium-high heat until butter is melted and water starts to simmer. Rapidly stir in the flour. Cook 1 minute, stirring constantly to prevent sticking. Remove from heat and allow to cool at least 3 minutes.

If the sauce pan is small, transfer to a larger bowl. Once cooled (still warm, but not enough to burn or cook the eggs) mix in eggs one at a time with electric mixer, then mix in cheese.

Place spoonfuls (~1 Tbsp) on a parchment-paper-covered cookie sheet. They do not spread much, so they can be pretty close. Bake 10 minutes, then turn oven down to 350 and continue baking for ~20 minutes, until golden brown and firm when tapped.

Cool on a wire rack a few minutes. Serve warm.

Makes 2-3 dozen.
