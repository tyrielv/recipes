# Garlic Butter Spread

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, softened
- 1 tsp garlic salt (OR 1 1/2 tsp freshly minced garlic + 1 teaspoon salt)
- 1 tsp parsley flakes

## Directions

With a hand mixer, blend all ingredients together. Spread on hot [French bread](french-bread.md) to make a tasty garlic bread!
