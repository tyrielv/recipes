# Hamburger Pot Pie (or "Meat Pie" as I grew up calling it.)

## Ingredients

**Hamburger Filling:**
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1/2 c onion, chopped
- 1 c cubed potatoes, slightly cooked
- 1 (10 oz) can tomato soup
- 1 (10 oz) can vegetable beef soup
- 1 (10 oz) can green beans
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp ground pepper
- 1/8 tsp dried oregano

**No-Fail Pie Crust:**
- 2 1/2 c flour
- 1 tsp garlic salt (if you're using this pie crust for anything else, use plain salt)
- 1 c shortening
- 1/4 c water (as cold as you can get it)
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp vinegar

## Directions

**For the filling:** In a large sauté pan, brown ground beef (and drain fat as it cooks). When beef is almost completely cooked, add onion and cook until tender. Add remaining ingredients and stir well. Set aside, and prepare pastry crust. Once the pastry is ready, pour in filling and place top crust on.

Bake at 425º for 13-15 minutes to brown crust, then turn heat down to 350º for 45 minutes or until golden brown in color.

**For the pie crust:** Sift together flour and salt, then cut in shortening. Set aside. Mix together all liquids, and stir into flour mixture. (Hint: Do not over-mix the dough! Don't even mix it in a mixer because it's too easy to over-do. I seriously just use a fork to mix it all together.)

Separate dough into two balls and roll out two thin pastry crusts. Bake as directed above.
