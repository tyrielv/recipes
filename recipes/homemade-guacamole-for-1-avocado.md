# Homemade Guacamole for 1 Avocado


## Ingredients

- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp minced dried onion
- 1/4 tsp dried cilantro
- 1/4 tsp rounded cumin
- 1/8 tsp salt (or more to taste)
- 1-2 tbsp sundried tomatoes, finely chopped
- 1/2-1 tsp lime juice (to help prevent browning)

## Directions

Mash avocado with fork and combine well with all ingredients. Allow to sit for at least half hour to soak in the seasonings.
