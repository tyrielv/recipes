# Homemade Ice Cream (Without Ice Cream Maker)

*Source: <https://www.tastemade.com/videos/no-churn-ice-cream-4-ways>*

---

## Ingredients

**For the vanilla ice cream base:**

- 2 cups whipping cream
- 1 can (14 ounces) condensed milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

**Optional ideas for making Cookies N Cream, Cookie Dough, or Mint ice creams:**

- *5 oreos, roughly chopped
- *about one batch [edible cookie dough](edible-cookie-dough.md), give or take, depending upon how much you like, plus coarsely chopped chocolate chips.
- *Swap out vanilla extract for peppermint extract, and add 7 York Peppermint Patties and 5 mint oreo cookies, roughly chopped.

---

## Instructions

Whip the whipping cream with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form. Set aside.

In a large bowl, combine the condensed milk and vanilla extract (or other flavor). Add a large dollop of whipped cream and mix until well combined. Add the remaining whipped cream and gently fold to combine.

Add the optional ingredients. Fold to combine.

Pour into a freezer-safe container and seal with a lid. Freeze for 6 hours, or until firm.

We made a combination of Cookies N Cream, and Cookie Dough (minus chocolate chips).
