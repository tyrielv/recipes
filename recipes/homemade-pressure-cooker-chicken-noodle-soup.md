# Homemade Pressure Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup chopped onion
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 8 cups chicken broth (or 8 cups of water and 8 tsp of chicken bouillon)
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (uncooked if using a pressure cooker; cooked and cubed if not using a pressure cooker)
- 12-16 oz egg noodles, rotelle pasta, or similar
- 2 cup sliced carrots
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

Place butter in cooking pot of electric pressure cooker\*. Select Sauté on a high heat. Once butter begins to sizzle, stir the chopped onions into the cooking pot. Sauté for about 1 to 2 minutes, until onions begin to soften and become translucent. Stir in the carrots and celery. Sauté vegetables for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. The vegetables will pick up some color, but keep stirring so they do not become too brown.

Toss chicken with kosher salt. Add chicken, basil, oregano, pepper, and chicken broth (or water and bouillon) to cooking pot. Lock lid in place. Select High Pressure and set timer for 5 minutes. When timer sounds wait 5 minutes and then use Quick Pressure Release to release pressure. When float valve drops, remove lid carefully, tilting away from you to allow steam to disperse.

Remove chicken pieces and when cool enough to handle, dice into bite-size pieces. Add chicken back to pot. Bring pot to a boil and add noodles. Cook noodles according to package directions. Once noodles have cooked, remove from heat and serve.

\*If you don't have a pressure cooker: In a large pot over medium-high heat, melt butter. Sauté onion for about 1-2 minutes in butter, then add celery and carrots to pan and cook until just tender (about 5 minutes). Pour in chicken broth, basil, oregano, and salt/pepper to taste. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes (or more for softer vegetables). Once vegetables are softened, bring back to a boil, add pasta, and cook according to package directions. Remove pan from heat and stir in cooked, cubed chicken.
