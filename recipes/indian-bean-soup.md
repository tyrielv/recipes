# Indian Bean Soup

## Ingredients

- 4 Tbl vegetable oil
- 2 med. onions, chopped
- 3 med potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 1 parsnip, peeled and cubed
- 1 turnip or rutabaga, peeled and cubed
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1-2 zucchini, sliced
- 1 green bell pepper, seeded and diced
- 2 cloves minced garlic
- 2 tsp ground coriander
- 1 Tbl paprika
- 1 Tbl mild curry paste
- 5 cups broth (vegetable or chicken)
- salt to taste
- 1 can (14-16 oz.) black beans, drained and rinsed

## Directions

Heat the vegetable oil over medium heat in a stock pot (or a very large sauce pan). Add the prepared vegetables except the zucchini and green pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, for 5 minutes. Add the garlic, ground coriander, paprika, and curry paste, and cook for 1 minute.

Add the broth. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer over low heat, stirring occasionally, for 25 minutes.

Stir in the black beans, zucchini and green peppers. Continue cooking 15 minutes. This is a good time to taste the soup and add salt if needed.

Thicken if desired either by blending up 1 cup of the soup (in a blender or food processor), or with corn starch. (We prefer corn starch.) Garnish with sour cream.

Serves 6-8.
