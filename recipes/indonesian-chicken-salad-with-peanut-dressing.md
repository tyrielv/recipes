# Indonesian Chicken Salad with Peanut Dressing

## Ingredients

- 2 Chicken breasts
- 5 red potatoes
- 10 oz. pineapple, diced
- 1 carrot, grated
- 1 cup bean sprouts (or substitute sugar snap peas)
- 4 green onions, chopped in 1/2-inch pieces
- 1 zucchini, cut into thin sticks
- 2 celery stalks, cut into thin sticks
- 1/2 cup peanuts

**Peanut Dressing:**
- 6 Tbl peanut butter
- 6 Tbl olive oil
- 2 Tbl soy sauce or teriyaki sauce
- 2 Tbl sesame oil
- 4 tsp lime juice
- 1 red chili, chopped, or equivalent in chili flakes, chili powder, or cayenne pepper. (Just enough to make it a bit spicy)

## Directions

Bake chicken at 350 for 20-25 minutes or until cooked. (I often cut them thinner so they'll cook faster.) When done, slice thinly.

Meanwhile, bring a pan of water to boil. Dice the potatoes, and boil for 10 minutes or until you can easily stab them with a fork. Drain the potatoes and transfer to a large salad bowl.

Add the other ingredients (pineapple, carrots, bean sprouts, green onions, zucchini, celery, and peanuts) and toss well.

Serves 4

**Peanut Dressing:** Whisk the olive oil and the peanut butter together, then mix in the other ingredients.
