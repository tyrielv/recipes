# Injudra

*Source: http://caloriecount.about.com/injudra-recipe-r78172*

## Ingredients

* 1/2 lb lentils
* 2 quarts water
* 1 cup brown rice
* 1 Tbl cumin
* 1.5-2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp pepper
* 4-5 onions, chopped
* 1/2 cup olive oil

## Directions

Saute onions in olive oil until dark brown (this will take the whole time the rice and lentils are cooking).

After the onions are started, boil lentils in water until starting to soften, about 10-15 minutes. Add rice and spices, and cook until the water is gone, about 60-90 minutes.

Mix onions into rice and lentils.
