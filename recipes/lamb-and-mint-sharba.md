# Lamb and Mint Sharba

*Recipe adapted from the America's Test Kitchen Mediterranean Cookbook.*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 lb lamb (substitute beef)
- salt and pepper
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped fine
- 4 plum tomatoes, cored and cut in 1/4-inch pieces, or 1 14oz can diced tomatoes
- 2 Tbsp tomato paste
- 1 1/2 tsp dried mint (substitute 1/8 tsp peppermint extract, or cilantro)
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- 7-10 C chicken broth
- 15 oz can chickpeas (subst. 3/4 C dry chickpeas, cooked)
- 1 C orzo or 6oz other small pasta

---

## Directions

Heat oven to 325 with oven rack in lower third.

Sear meat in olive oil, in dutch oven on medium-high heat. Transfer to plate.

Cook onion over medium heat until softened (5 min).

Add tomatoes and cook until softened and juice has evaporated (2 min).

Stir in tomato paste, turmeric, paprika, cinnamon, cumin, 1/2 of the mint, 1 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper.

Add broth and bring to boil.

Stir in chickpeas. Add meat. Cover and cook in oven until meat is tender, about 1 hour.

Remove meat, let cool slightly, then cut to bite-size pieces.

Meanwhile add orzo and simmer 10-12 minutes, until tender.

Stir in shredded meat and remaining mint.
