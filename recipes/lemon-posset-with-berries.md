# Lemon Posset with Berries

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/8716-lemon-posset-with-berries)*

## Ingredients

- 2 C heavy cream
- 2/3 C sugar
- 1 Tbl lemon zest and 6 Tbl lemon juice (~2 lemons)
- 1 1/2 C (or to taste) fresh blueberries or raspberries

## Directions

1. Whisk together cream, sugar, and lemon zest in a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring frequently.
2. Continue boiling until reduced to 2 C, stirring frequently.
3. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice.
4. Allow to sit for 20 minutes.
5. Pour through fine mesh strainer to remove lemon zest.
6. Portion into 6 ramekins. (~1/3 C each)
7. Chill uncovered at least 3 hours. (Can be covered after 3 hours)
8. Serve with berries.
