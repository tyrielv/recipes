# Lentil Fritters

## Ingredients

- 1 cup dry lentils
- 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp. oregano
- 1/2 tsp. chili powder
- 3/4 tsp. salt
- 4 1/2 Tbl flour
- 1 4oz. can diced green chilis

## Directions

Cook the lentils according to package directions (boil for 20-25 minutes). Drain and rinse.

Combine lentils with remaining ingredients and puree in a blender or food processor, or with a stick blender.

Brush frying pan with olive oil and bring to medium-high heat. Spoon out lentils onto pan and press flat - we do about 3" diameter round fritters. Fry for two minutes on each side.

Repeat (adding more oil as necessary) until all the fritters are cooked.

Goes well with sour cream and BBQ sauce.

Makes enough for 2-3 people.
