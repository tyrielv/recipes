# Macaroni and Cheese


For 6 oz of uncooked macaroni pasta (or similar type pasta), cooked and drained:

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2-3 oz shredded cheddar (to taste — frankly I don't usually measure it, I just eyeball it) or other preferred cheese

## Directions

Make a roux by melting the butter in a small saucepan and whisking flour into butter when bubbling. Whisk in salt and milk. Bring to a low boil and simmer until white sauce is thickened. Remove from heat, and stir in cheese until melted and mixed completely into the sauce. Pour over cooked and drained pasta and stir to combine. Serve immediately.
