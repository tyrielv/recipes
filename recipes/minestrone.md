# Minestrone

*Adapted from "The Classic Italian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 3 Tbl butter
* 1/2 C olive oil
* 1 C sliced onions
* 1 C diced carrots
* 1 C diced celery
* 2 C diced potatoes
* 2 C diced zucchini (~2 medium zucchini)
* 1 C cut green beans
* 3 C shredded cabbage
* 2 Tbl chicken or vegetable bouillon (6 cubes)
* 4 cups water (use 7 if doubling)
* 1 parmesan crust (optional)
* 1 14oz can diced tomatoes
* 1 can navy beans (or 3/4 C dry beans, cooked)

## Directions

In a large stock pot (at least 6 qts), heat oil and butter over medium-low.

Add the vegetables (onions through cabbage) in order, as they are chopped (don't pre-chop them all beforehand), stirring after each one. Continue cooking for 5 minutes. Add the broth, parmesan crust, and tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and boil gently for ~3 hours. Add more water by the 1/2 C if needed. 15 minutes before the end, add the beans.

The original recipe called for 1/3 C grated Parmesan to be stirred in just before serving. We find that this leads to Parmesan coating the bottom of the pan and the serving utensils, so we prefer to add Parmesan to taste to individual bowls at the table.
