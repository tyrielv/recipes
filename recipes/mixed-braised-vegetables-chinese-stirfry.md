# Mixed Braised Vegetables (Chinese Stirfry)

*Adapted from "The Complete Asian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 2 Tbl peanut or vegetable oil
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* 1 clove garlic, crushed or minced
* 1 tsp minced fresh ginger
* 1 1/2 lb. thinly sliced vegetables (such as cabbage, cauliflower, green onions, bean sprouts, carrots, broccoli, bell pepper, etc.)
* 1/2 C hot water or stock
* 1 Tbl oyster sauce (or substitute 1 Tbl fish sauce and 1/4 tsp sugar)
* 2 tsp soy sauce
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 MSG (optional)
* 2 tsp cornstarch
* 1 Tbl cold water

## Directions

Heat oil in wok with garlic and ginger. Add vegetables and stirfry for 2 minutes. Add hot water, sauces, and salt (and MSG if using). Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer 4 minutes. Mix cornstarch and cold water. Push the vegetables to the side, and pour the cornstarch mixture into the sauce. Stir until thickened. Toss vegetables in sauce, and serve immediately with white rice.
