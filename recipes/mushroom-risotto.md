# Mushroom Risotto

*Adapted from http://allrecipes.com/recipe/gourmet-mushroom-risotto/*

## Ingredients

* 1/2 to 1 lb mushrooms, sliced
* 1 onion, diced
* 3 tbsp olive oil divided into 2
* 1 c white or arborito rice
* 4 c broth, simmering in small sauce pan
* 3 tbsp butter
* 1/4 c grated parmesan

## Directions

Fry mushrooms in 1 1/2 tbsp oil until soft and browned. Remove and set aside. Saute onions in remaining 1 1/2 tbsp. oil for 3-5 minutes until translucent. Add rice and stir-fry for a couple of minutes until translucent. Gradually add broth, stirring frequently, adding only enough at a time to barely coat rice. If rice is not done when out of broth, continue with water. Stir in butter, mushrooms, and parmesan.
