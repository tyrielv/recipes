# Naan Bread

*Adapted from <https://www.budgetbytes.com/naan/>*

---

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 C flour plus more for kneading/rolling
- 2 tsp instant yeast
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 C water
- 1/3 C plain yogurt (greek is fine)
- 1/4 C olive oil
- 1 egg
- butter

---

## Directions

Whisk together dry ingredients, then stir in wet ingredients with a spoon.

Dump out onto a floured surface and knead 3 minutes, adding flour as necessary to prevent sticking.

The dough should be soft but not sticky.

Dust with flour, cover with a towel and let rise for 1-2 hours.

Heat griddle over medium.

Divide dough into 8. Roll each portion out flat. Cook until browned on each side (1-3 minutes), then brush with butter.
