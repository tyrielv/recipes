# One Skillet Lasagna

*Adapted from [Cooking at Home with America's Test Kitchen](https://www.amazon.com/gp/offer-listing/0936184892/)*

## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium onion, minced
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 3 cloves minced garlic
- 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1 lb ground beef
- 10 lasagna noodles
- 28 oz canned diced tomatoes, plus enough water to make a quart
- 8 oz canned tomato sauce
- 1 Tbsp dry basil
- 1 or 2 oz grated Parmesan
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 Cup or more of ricotta

## Directions

Heat 1 tbsp oil over medium in large skillet. Mince 1 medium onion and add with 1/2 tsp salt to the pan, cooking until beginning to brown (about 5 minutes). Stir in 3 cloves of garlic and 1/8 tsp of pepper flakes. (For kids who are picky about chunks, purée diced tomatoes, water, and onion after onion has sautéed long enough.)

Add 1 lb ground beef, and cook until browned. Meanwhile, break 10 lasagna noodles into 2 inch pieces. Scatter pasta over the top, without stirring.

Pour one 28oz can (plus water to bring it to 1 quart) of diced tomatoes, one 8oz can tomato sauce, and 1 tbsp basil over the pasta. Cover, bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and simmer 20-30 minutes until pasta is tender.

Remove from heat. Gently stir in 1-2oz grated parmesan. Salt and pepper to taste. Add 1 cup or more of ricotta by tablespoonfuls, then cover and let it soften from the heat for 5 minutes.
