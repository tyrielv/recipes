# Panang Tofu Curry

*Adapted from [Panang Beef Curry](panang-beef-curry.md)*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 onion, diced
- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 Tbsp red curry paste
- Fresh ginger about the size of a thumb or two, grated
- 4-6 cloves minced garlic
- 1 14 oz can coconut milk
- 4 tsp fish sauce
- 2 tsp sugar
- 14-20 oz firm or extra firm tofu, pressed and cut into bite size cubes.
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1 Tbsp lime juice
- 1/3 C nuts (eg cashews or peanuts), chopped

---

## Directions

Cook onion in oil over medium-low until translucent and starting to brown, about 15-20 minutes.

Stir in curry paste and continue cooking for 5 minutes.

Stir in ginger and garlic. Continue cooking 1 minute.

Stir in coconut milk, fish sauce, sugar, and tofu.

Bring to a simmer over medium, then reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered for 10-20 minutes, until thickened.

Stir in lemon juice, lime juice, and nuts.

Serve with rice and stir fried vegetables.
