# Peanut Butter Chocolate No-Bake Cookies

*Source: <https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchen/peanut-butter-chocolate-no-bake-cookies-recipe-2015085>*

**Yield:** about 60 cookies

---

## Ingredients

- 2 c sugar
- 1/2 c milk
- 1 stick unsalted butter (8 tbsp)
- 1/4 c unsweetened cocoa powder
- 3 c old-fashioned rolled oats
- 1 c smooth peanut butter
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- large pinch kosher salt

---

## Directions

Line a baking sheet with wax or parchment paper.

Bring sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa to a boil in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally, then let boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat.

Add oats, peanut butter, vanilla, and salt. Stir to combine. Drop teaspoonfulls of the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet and let sit at room temperature until cooled and hardened, about 30 minutes.

Refrigerate in airtight container for up to 3 days.
