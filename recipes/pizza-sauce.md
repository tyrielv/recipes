# Pizza Sauce

*Loosely based on Tomato Sauce #3 from "Classic Italian Cooking"*

---

## Ingredients

- 28 oz can whole tomatoes
- 6 oz can tomato paste
- 1/2 C olive oil
- 1/4 C diced onion
- ~2 tbsp of whole peeled garlic cloves (about 3-6 depending on size)
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp dry basil
- 1/2 tsp dry oregano

---

## Directions

Stir ingredients together in a 2 or 3 qt sauce pan. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered for 1 hour. Purée.
