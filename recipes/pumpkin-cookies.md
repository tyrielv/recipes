# Pumpkin Cookies

## The easy way to make pumpkin cookies:

### Ingredients

* 14 oz can pumpkin
* 1 box spice cake mix
* 1+ C chocolate chips

### Directions

Mix together. Bake tablespoonfuls on a greased cookie sheet for 12-18 minutes at 350 ºF

## From scratch:

### Ingredients

* 1 C brown sugar
* 1/2 C sugar
* 1 stick softened butter
* 1 egg
* 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
* 14 oz can pumpkin
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1/2 tsp nutmeg
* 1/2 tsp ginger
* 1/4 tsp cloves
* 2 1/4 to 2 1/2 C flour (less for flatter cookies)
* 1+ C chocolate chips

### Directions

Mix sugars and butter with electric mixer until smooth. Mix in all other ingredients except flour and chocolate chips. Gradually mix in the flour, switching to a spoon when it gets too thick for the electric mixer. Stir in chocolate chips. Bake tablespoonfuls on a greased cookie sheet for 12-18 minutes at 350 ºF
