# Red Beans and Rice

*Adapted from http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/emeril-lagasse/new-orleans-style-red-beans-and-rice-recipe.html*

## Ingredients

* 1 lb dried red beans, cooked
* 2 Tbl oil
* 1 large onion, chopped
* 1 bell pepper, chopped
* 3 stalks celery, chopped
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (I only use about 1/8 tsp for the kids' sake)
* 1/4 tsp black pepper
* 1 tsp dry thyme
* 2 bay leaves
* 1 lb kielbasa or other sausage, chopped
* 3 Tbl minced garlic
* 2-3 C water
* Steamed rice (if mixing in, cook 1 1/2 C with 3/4 tsp salt and about 1 tbsp butter)

## Directions

Saute onion, bell pepper, celery, and spices for 5 minutes. Add kielbasa and continue cooking 5 minutes. Add bay leaves, garlic, beans, and water. Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, for at least 40 minutes up to a few hours. Add water if necessary, but it should be thick and not watery. Serve over rice.
