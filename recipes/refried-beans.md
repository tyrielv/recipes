# Refried Beans

## Ingredients

* 1 c dry pinto beans, cooked and drained (reserve liquid--optional)
* 1/4 c shortening or similar
* c reserved liquid or water
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/4 c picante salsa or to taste
* 1/4-1/2 tsp onion powder
* 1/4-1/2 tsp garlic powder

## Directions

Mash the beans into paste (or use a blender) Mix in liquid, salsa, and seasonings and set aside. Heat pan to medium-high and melt shortening. Add remaining ingredients and simmer until desired thickness. (Note: bean mixture will thicken as it cools, so don't over-thicken.)
