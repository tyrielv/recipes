# Ricotta Gnocchi

*Adapted from <https://www.seriouseats.com/2015/03/technique-fresh-ricotta-gnocchi-fast.html>*

---

## Ingredients

- 15 oz container of ricotta, preferably without added gums (Walmart's organic brand works)
- 1.5 oz grated parmesan
- 5.25 oz flour, plus more for rolling out
- 2 eggs
- salt to taste (start with 1/4 tsp)
- 2-3 cups pasta sauce

---

## Directions

Bring a pot of 3-4 quarts of salted water to boil.

Press ricotta between two layers of paper towels to remove excess liquid. There should be about 12 oz left.

Mix ricotta, parmesan, 5.25 oz flour, eggs, and salt to taste in a mixing bowl. It will be sticky.

On a lightly floured surface, divide into quarters, then divide each quarter into 3rds.

Roll each section into a rope about 3/4" inches thick, then cut into 1" pieces. Add to a floured baking sheet and roll around to coat (so they don't stick to each other). While rolling out, be conservative with the flour - it rolls best if still slightly sticky. If there is too much flour it will slide instead.

Add all at once to boiling water. Cook until it all floats for 30 seconds, about 3 minutes total.

Drain, then stir in sauce.
