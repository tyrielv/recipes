# Shredded Chicken Tacos

## Ingredients

- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breast (or about 1 lb)
- 3 tbsp [taco seasoning mix](taco-seasoning-mix.md)
- 1 c water

## Directions

Pre-heat oven to 400ºF and bake chicken breast for about 25-35 minutes or until cooked (internal temperature should be 180ºF) and no longer pink on inside. While still warm, cut chicken into halves or thirds, and put in a Bosch mixing bowl with the Cookie Paddles, and turn to speed one until chicken is shredded. If you don't have a Bosch mixer, you can use two forks to shred the chicken apart (although this takes much longer).

Place the shredded chicken in a microwave-safe bowl. Add taco seasoning mix and water to the chicken and stir well. Microwave seasoned chicken for 1 minute at a time on high, stirring in between minutes, until thickened.

Serve on [flour tortillas](flour-tortillas.md).

I also like to serve with sour cream, guacamole, shredded lettuce, shredded cheese, salsa or [taco sauce](taco-sauce.md), black beans, rice, and anything else you think might be good on a taco!
