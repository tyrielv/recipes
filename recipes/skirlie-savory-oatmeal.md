# Skirlie (Savory Oatmeal)

*Adapted from http://blog.bobsredmill.com/recipes/skirlie/*

## Ingredients

* 1/4 cup steel cut oats (regular rolled oats would work as well, too, just make sure to cook it for less time)
* 1/2 cup broth + more water as needed
* 1/8 tsp onion powder
* 1/8 tsp garlic powder
* 1/4 tsp dried rosemary
* 1/4 tsp dried sage
* 1/4 tsp dried thyme
* Ground pepper to taste

## Directions

Combine all ingredients in a small sauce pan on the stove and mix well. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for about 20 minutes or until oats are cooked through. Add water as necessary so it doesn't get too dry.

For a sweeter yet still savory flavor, use a about 2 tbsp of chopped, fresh onion and caramelize while oats are cooking. Stir in at the end.
