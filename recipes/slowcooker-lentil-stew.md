# Slowcooker Lentil Stew

*Adapted from [Whole Foods Market](https://www.wholefoodsmarket.com/recipe/slow-cooker-lentil-stew-polenta)*

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Cups brown lentils
- 2 onions, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 4 cloves of garlic, sliced or minced
- 28oz canned diced tomatoes
- 1 tsp dry oregano
- 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 Cups vegetable or chicken broth
- 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

## Directions

Mix lentils, onions, bell pepper, and garlic in slow cooker.

Pour tomatoes over top.

Sprinkle oregano, pepper flakes, and salt over top.

Pour in broth and vinegar.

Cook in slow cooker on low for 7-8 hours.

## Notes

Goes well with polenta.
