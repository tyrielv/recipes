# Spaghetti Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup onion, chopped
- 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (12 ounces) can tomato sauce
- 1 (6 1/2 ounces) can tomato paste
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon basil
- salt and pepper, to taste
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- ground beef, browned*
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

In large skillet heat oil, onion and garlic until tender. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper and sugar. Simmer 20 minutes. Stir in browned ground beef.

Pour tomato sauce over cooked spaghetti. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.

## Notes

*I typically brown the ground beef while the sauce is simmering. LEAVE IT OUT if you don't want a meat sauce!
