# Spinach and Ricotta Gnocchi

*From "The Classic Italian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 1 Tbl finely chopped onion
* 2 Tbl butter
* 2 Tbl finely chopped ham
* 1 10 oz. package of frozen spinach, thawed
* Salt as needed
* 3/4 C ricotta
* 2/3 cup (plus more as needed) flour
* 2 egg yolks
* 1 cup grated Parmesan
* 1/4 tsp nutmeg

## Directions

Saute onions in butter until pale gold.

Stir in ham.

Stir in spinach and 1/4 tsp salt. Saute 5-6 minutes. (Butter will all get absorbed)

Transfer to mixing bowl. Mix in ricotta and flour. Mix in egg yolks, parmesan, and nutmeg. Salt to taste.

Make into 1/2 inch pellets. Dust hands with flour frequently to prevent sticking.

Cook in one of three ways:

1. "Gratinéed": Preheat oven to 375º. Drop into boiling salted water (4 qts with 1 1/2 Tbl salt) "a few at one time." Return to a boil and boil for 2-3 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and put in greased baking dish. Repeat until all gnocchi are boiled. Melt 4 1/2 Tbl butter and pour over gnocchi. Sprinkle 1/2 C grated parmesan. Bake on top rack of oven for 5 minutes.

2. With tomato sauce - boil as in #1, then serve with a tomato sauce (e.g. the [tomato and cream sauce](tomato-and-cream-sauce-for-pasta.md)) and grated parmesan.

3. In broth: Bring 2 quarts homemade broth to a boil. Drop in all gnocchi, return to a boil, then boil 3-4 minutes. Serve as a soup.
