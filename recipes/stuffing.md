# Stuffing

## Ingredients

* 1/4 C onion, diced or minced
* 1 stalk celery, diced or minced
* 4 Tbl butter
* 1/2 tsp minced garlic
* 3/4 tsp sage
* 1/2 tsp thyme
* 1 Tbl parsley
* 1 1/2 tsp bouillon
* 4 C dried bread, chopped small (1/4" cubes)
* 1 - 1 1/2 C boiling water

## Directions

Sauté onion and celery in butter 10 minutes. Stir in garlic, herbs, and bouillon. Stir in bread. Slowly stir in boiling water a bit at a time until moist through.
