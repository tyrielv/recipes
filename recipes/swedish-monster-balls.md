# Swedish Monster Balls

## Ingredients

- 1/2 c butter, cold
- 2-3 tbsp cocoa
- 3 deciliters oatmeal (about 1 1/3 cups)
- 1 deciliter sugar (.42 cups--I usually do just under a 1/2 cup)
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- (if too dry can add milk)
- Shredded coconut for coating

## Directions

Mix up (easiest to do by hand), roll in coconut, refrigerate 1 hour.
