# Sweet Potato and Onion Soup

## Ingredients

- 2 Tbl vegetable oil
- 5-6 cups diced sweet potato or yam (about 2 large)
- 1 carrot, diced
- 2 onions, sliced
- 2 cloves minced garlic
- 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
- 1 1/4 cups orange juice
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp dry cilantro (or 2 Tbl chopped fresh cilantro)
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan and add the sweet potatoes, carrot, onions, and garlic. Saute for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.

Add chicken broth, orange juice, and dried cilantro (if using fresh cilantro, don't add it yet). Bring to a boil.

Reduce heat, cover and cook for 20 minutes.

Puree. The easiest way is with a stick/immersion blender, but you can also do several cups at a time in a regular blender or food processor.

Stir in yogurt and fresh cilantro (if using fresh). Add salt and pepper to taste.
