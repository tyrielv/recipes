# Taco Sauce

This is a very easy recipe to make. Here's what happened when we made it. I threw all the ingredients in the sauce pan. I asked someone to simmer it for me for the 15-20 minutes because I had to leave the house for a while to run an errand. I fully expected them to babysit the sauce on the stove, or at least stir it every once in a while during the simmering time. When I got home, the sauce was done perfectly and cooling on the stove-top, and so I assumed someone had kept a good eye on it while I had been gone. During dinner, it was announced all they did was turn the stove on, stir the sauce once, and leave to go do other things until returning 15 or 20 minutes later. (I don't recommend ignoring things on the stove like this so you don't accidentally burn them. We lucked out.) Anyway, the sauce turned out delicious!

## Ingredients

- 8 oz can tomato sauce
- 1/3 c water
- 1/4 tsp chili powder
- 1 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1 1/2 tsp dry minced onions
- 1 tbsp white vinegar
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp garlic salt
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

## Directions

Combine all ingredients in sauce pan and stir well. Simmer at a very low temperature for about 15-20 minutes. Remove and cool. May be kept in refrigerator for several days.
