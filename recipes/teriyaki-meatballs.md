# Teriyaki Meatballs

*Adapted from "The Best of America's Test Kitchen 2017"*

---

## Ingredients

- 6 oz mushrooms, chopped coarse (stemmed if using shiitake)
- 1 1/2 C chopped green cabbage
- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 3 cloves minced garlic
- 1 Tbsp grated fresh ginger
- 1 lb ground meat (turkey, pork, beef, chicken)
- 1/2 C panko or breadcrumbs
- 4 green onions, minced
- 3 Tbsp prepared [teriyaki sauce](teriyaki-sauce.md)
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- Additional teriyaki sauce (~1 C)

---

## Directions

Preheat the oven to 400º F.

Pulse mushrooms and cabbage in a food processor until finely chopped. (Or mince them with a knife if you don't have a food processor.)

Sauté oil, mushrooms, salt and pepper in a skillet over medium high until lightly browned, 6-8 minutes. Add garlic and ginger and cook for another 30 seconds. Remove from heat and let cool.

Prepare teriyaki sauce while mushrooms and cabbage are cooling.

In a medium bowl, combine mushroom and cabbage mixture, meat, bread crumbs, green onions, 3 tbsp prepared teriyaki sauce, egg, and sesame oil. Form into ~40 meatballs, ~1Tbsp each, on a greased or parchment-lined cookie sheet. Meatballs will be very soft in texture.

Bake for 10 minutes. Rotate the pan, then bake for 10 minutes more. Toss gently with remaining teriyaki sauce in a serving bowl. Garnish with sesame seeds (optional), and serve over steamed rice.
