# Thai Peanut Chicken

## Ingredients

- 1/2 C water
- 1/4 C soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 C peanut butter
- 8 tsp white wine vinegar
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper (or paprika to make less spicy)
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips OR 1 14oz block extra firm tofu, cubed
- 3 tbsp minced garlic
- 1.5 tbsp ground ginger or grated fresh ginger
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 2-3 heads of broccoli
- optional: peanuts or cashews

## Directions

1. In a small bowl, stir together water, soy sauce, sugar, peanut butter, vinegar, and cayenne pepper. Set aside.
2. Heat oil in a skillet or wok over high heat. Add chicken or tofu, onion, garlic, and ginger and cook, stirring constantly until chicken is golden brown on outside (about 5 minutes).
3. Reduce heat to medium. Add broccoli and the peanut butter mixture. Cook, stirring frequently, until broccoli is tender and chicken is cooked through, about 15-20 minutes.

Serve over rice and top with peanuts or cashews.
