# Thai Peanut Noodles

*Adapted from <https://www.noracooks.com/peanut-noodles/>*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 package (14oz) firm or extra-firm tofu
- 1 package (14oz) rice noodles
- 1 C peanut butter
- 1 C water
- 1/2 C soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp minced garlic
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 C lime juice
- 6 Tbsp brown sugar if using no-sugar-added peanut butter or 4Tbsp if using sugary peanut butter
- 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
- Broccoli, about 1 large or 3 small heads, cut into very small florets.

---

## Directions

Drain tofu. Optionally press, otherwise just leave to dry on some paper towels.

Bring ~7 cups of water (a full kettle) to a boil, then add to rice noodles in a large bowl or pot. Let sit 10 minutes, then drain.

Meanwhile, whisk together peanut butter, water, soy sauce, garlic, onion powder, lime juice, and brown sugar in a medium bowl.

Chop tofu into small pieces, 1/4" to 1/2" cubes, and stir into sauce.

Heat oil over medium heat in a large skillet or frying pan. Sauté broccoli for ~5-10 minutes, stirring frequently, until tender.

Stir in sauce/tofu and noodles. Heat until bubbling while stirring occasionally.
