# Tofu and Bean Taco Filling

*Adapted from <https://www.wellplated.com/tofu-tacos/>*

---

## Ingredients

- About 1 lb firm or extra-firm tofu, drained. (No need to press or pre-bake.)
- 2 Tbsp oil, divided
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1 14oz can beans (or 3/4 C dry beans or lentils, cooked)
- 1/2 C salsa
- 2 Tbsp nutritional yeast

---

## Directions

With a fork, stir 1 Tbsp oil and seasonings into tofu, aiming for ground-beef texture. Stir in beans, salsa, and yeast.

Heat remaining 1 Tbsp oil in skillet on medium high. Add the tofu and cook until most moisture has cooked off, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
