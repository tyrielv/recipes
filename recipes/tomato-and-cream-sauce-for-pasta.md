# Tomato and Cream Sauce for Pasta

*From "The Classic Italian Cookbook"*

## Ingredients

* 28 oz can whole tomatoes
* 11 Tbl butter (1 and 1/3 sticks)
* 1/4 C each of chopped carrot, celery, and onion
* Salt to taste (start with 2 tsp)
* 1/4 tsp sugar
* 1/2 to 2/3 C heavy cream

## Directions

Add all ingredients except cream to a sauce pan. Simmer (don't boil) on low for 1 hour, uncovered, stirring occasionally. Purée with stick blender. Stir in cream and cook 1 minute more. Salt to taste.

Makes enough for 8-10 servings of pasta. Freezes well.
