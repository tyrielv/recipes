# Tomato Soup (1 quart)

*Recipe based on "Have Apron, Will Travel" (family heirloom cookbook). Originally written to make a large batch for canning (half bushel of tomatoes). This recipe has been cut down to only make 1 quart.*

Great with [Cheese Dumplings](cheese-dumplings-for-tomato-soup.md).

---

## Ingredients

- 28 oz canned whole tomatoes
- 1/2 large onion, diced
- 1/2 bell pepper, diced
- dash of pepper
- 1 tsp salt (or more, to taste)
- 2-4 tsp sugar, to taste
- 1-2 tbsp vinegar, to taste

---

## Directions

Simmer vegetables 20 minutes.

Purée.

Add seasonings and continue cooking for 5 minutes.

If not using for [dumplings](cheese-dumplings-for-tomato-soup.md), add a roux of 2 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp flour to thicken.
