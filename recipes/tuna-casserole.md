# Tuna Casserole


## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 cup milk + 1/2 cup
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 4 oz shredded cheddar
- 3 cups noodles, cooked
- 6.5-7 oz canned tuna, drained
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup sliced celery
- 1/3 cup chopped onion
- 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt

## Directions

Make a roux by melting the butter in a saucepan on medium heat, then whisking in flour.

Add 1 cup of milk and salt. Simmer until thickened.

Add remaining 1/2 cup milk and cheddar. Stir until cheese has melted.

Combine noodles, tuna, mayo, celery, onion, bell pepper, salt, and cheese sauce.

Turn into casserole dish.

Top with slivered almonds.

Bake uncovered at 425⁰F for about 20 minutes. Serves 6.
