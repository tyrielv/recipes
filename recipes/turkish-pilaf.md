# Turkish Pilaf

*Adapted from http://www.food.com/recipe/savory-turkish-pilaf-91222*

## Ingredients

* 4 Tbl butter
* 1/2 lb lamb or beef, cut into small pieces (1/2" cubes or 1/4" strips)
* 3 medium onions, chopped
* 1/4 C chopped walnuts
* 2 C uncooked white rice
* 14 oz can diced tomatoes
* 1/4 C craisins or raisins
* 2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp pepper
* 1/2 tsp sage
* 1/4 tsp allspice
* 4 C boiling beef bouillon (or 4 C boiling water and bouillon cubes)
* 1 tsp parsley

## Directions

Sauté meat in butter over medium heat until browned. Remove meat from pan and set aside. Sauté onion in leftover butter until soft. Add nuts and rice. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Add tomato, (c)raisins, seasonings, and bouillon or water. Cover and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to low and cook until rice is done, 20-30 minutes. Stir in meat and heat through.
