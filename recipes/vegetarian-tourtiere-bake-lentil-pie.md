# Vegetarian Tourtiere Bake ("Lentil Pie")

*Adapted from <https://www.foodnetwork.ca/recipe/vegetarian-tourtiere-bake/14043/>*

---

## Ingredients

- 1/2 Cup lentils
- 1 Tbsp butter (or olive oil)
- 8 oz mushrooms, chopped
- 1 ribs celery, thinly sliced
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 small potato, peeled and diced
- 1 clove minced garlic
- 1/2 Cup vegetable broth
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1/4 tsp thyme
- 1/4 tsp marjoram
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1/4 tsp allspice
- 1/3 C chopped walnuts
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 [Pie crust](hamburger-pot-pie.md)

---

## Directions

Preheat oven to 425º F.

Boil lentils in 2 cups of water with 1/2 tsp salt until tender, 20-25 minutes.

Meanwhile sauté mushrooms, celery, onion, and potato in butter until onions are starting to soften, 10-15 minutes.

Add garlic, broth, soy sauce, and seasonings to the vegetables. Bring to a boil and continue boiling until liquid is mostly evaporated.

Drain lentils.

Stir in walnuts, eggs, and lentils.

Make into a pie.

Bake 15 minutes, then turn temperature down to 350 and bake 30 more minutes.
