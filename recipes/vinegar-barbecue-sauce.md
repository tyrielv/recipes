# Vinegar Barbecue Sauce

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/5025-lexington-vinegar-barbecue-sauce)*

## Ingredients

- 1/4 Cup apple cider vinegar
- 2 Tbsp ketchup
- 2 Tbsp water
- 2 Tbsp de-fatted meat drippings
- 3/4 tsp sugar
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper flakes
- 1/8 tsp black pepper

## Directions

Whisk together.
