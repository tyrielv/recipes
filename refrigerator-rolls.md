# Refrigerator Rolls*

## Ingredients and Directions

- 1 tbsp yeast
- 1/3 cup warm water
- 1 tbsp sugar

Dissolve and let stand.

- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup butter or margarine

Scald milk with sugar and salt. Remove from heat, add butter cut in slices, and set aside to cool for 5-10 mins.

- 3 eggs, beaten

Add eggs to milk mixture and then add yeast mixture to milk mixture.

- 4 1/2 cups flour

Mix in flour (mom uses her bread mixer) -- the batter is really sticky. Put batter in a bowl, spread melted butter over the top and cover with plastic wrap. Chill overnight (can keep up to one week).

Divide dough in half. Roll into 12" circle. Cut into 16 wedges (like a pie). Roll up piece by piece by starting with the big end first. Put on a cookie sheet that has been sprayed with non-stick spray. Let rise in a cool place for an hour or so.

Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 mins. Yield: 32

## Notes

*My mom made these every year at Thanksgiving when I was growing up (assuming we weren't breaking tradition and making pizza or something instead). They are still my favorite roll!
