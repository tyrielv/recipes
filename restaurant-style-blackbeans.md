# Restaurant-Style Blackbeans

## Ingredients

- 1 c dry blackbeans
- 4 c water
- 1 tsp salt + 1/2-3/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder

**Slurry:**

- 1/2-1 c reserved liquid after cooking beans
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp water

---

## Directions

1. In pressure cooker, add black beans, water, 1 tsp salt, onion powder, and garlic powder. Cook on high for 37 minutes. Natural pressure release.
2. Reserve 3/4 - 1 c of the liquid after cooking and set aside. Drain beans (DO NOT RINSE).
3. Return beans to pot with reserved liquid and add 1/2 - 3/4 tsp salt (to taste). Bring to a boil.
4. Meanwhile, make a cornstarch slurry with 1 tbsp cornstarch and 1 tbsp water. Stir into pot with beans.
5. Simmer until beans reach desired thickness/consistency. Remove from heat and serve.
