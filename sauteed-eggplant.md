# Sautéed Eggplant

*Adapted from "The Classic Italian Cookbook"*

For every 3 servings:

## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 lb of eggplant (1 large)
* Salt as needed
* 1/2 tsp minced garlic
* 2 1/2 Tbl olive oil
* 1 Tbl parsley
* Black pepper, to taste

## Directions

Peel and dice eggplant. Place in colander and salt liberally, tossing to ensure all are salted. Let stand for 30-60 minutes (the salt draws out excess moisture). Remove from colander and blot with paper towel to dry.

Heat 2 Tbl olive oil in skillet over medium heat. Sauté garlic and eggplant for 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Add remaining olive oil and saute for 10 minutes. Stir in parsley, add salt and pepper to taste. Continue cooking over medium-low heat until eggplant is tender (but still firm), ~20-30 minutes.
