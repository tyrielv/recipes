# Shrimp Alfredo

## Ingredients

**Alfredo Sauce\*:**
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

**Shrimp:**
- 1 tbsp butter or frying oil
- 1/2 lb raw shrimp, peeled and deveined

## Directions

In a saucepan, melt butter. Add flour and salt and mix well until lumps are gone. Add milk; bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until thickened. Add cheese, and stir until completely melted. If sauce is too thick, add a bit of milk, and mix well.

Melt butter in frying pan over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and fry until shrimp is pink and tender.

Serve over cooked pasta, and enjoy! Goes well with fresh [garlic bread](french-bread.md).

## Notes

\*For a more classic Alfredo sauce (similar to what they serve at Red Lobster):

- 1 pint of Half and Half
- 1 stick of unsalted butter
- 2 tbsp cream cheese\*\*
- 1/2 - 3/4 c Parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp. garlic powder

In a saucepan melt butter, then add the cream cheese. When the cream cheese is softened add the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder; stir well. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 - 20 minutes on low. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

\*\*True Alfredo Sauce does not have cream cheese (as you may have noticed), but it does help thicken the sauce up. You can leave it out without much difference to the taste, just be prepared to simmer the sauce a little longer to thicken it.
