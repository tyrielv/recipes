# Sicilian Supper

## Ingredients

**Meat layer:**
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1/2 c chopped onion OR 1/2 tbsp onion powder
- 1 (6oz) can tomato paste
- 3/4 c water
- 1/2 tsp salt
- dried basil and oregano flakes to taste (not in the original recipe, but I like the Italian flavor it adds)

**Noodle layer:**
- 3/4 c milk
- 8oz cream cheese
- 1/4 c grated Parmesan
- 1/2 tsp garlic salt
- 2 c uncooked, thin egg noodles (cooked)--use more or less as necessary for other types of noodles)

## Directions

Brown the meat (draining the fat as it cooks), and then add the remaining ingredients. Let simmer for 5 minutes.

Heat and mix milk and cream cheese until smooth. Stir in noodles, Parmesan, and garlic salt.

Spoon meat and noodle mixtures into alternating rows in a casserole baking dish. Bake at 350ºF for 20 minutes, or until heated through (bubbly). Sprinkle top with Parmesan cheese, and serve immediately.

Makes one 8"x8" casserole dish.
