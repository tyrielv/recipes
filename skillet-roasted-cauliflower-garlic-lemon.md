# Skillet-Roasted Cauliflower with Garlic and Lemon

*Adapted from "Best of America's Test Kitchen 2017"*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower (2 lbs)
- 1/4 C bread crumbs
- 5 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil, divided (2 and 3 Tbsp)
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1 clove garlic
- Zest of 1 lemon, plus the lemon cut in wedges for serving

---

## Directions

Cut out leaves and stem of cauliflower. Cut florets into 1 1/2" pieces.

Heat 2 Tbsp oil, cauliflower, salt, and pepper in a skillet with lid over medium high. When it starts sizzling, cover and let cook for 5 minutes.

Uncover and cook until turning golden brown, about 12 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Push cauliflower to edges. Add remaining 3 Tbsp oil, garlic, and lemon zest in center, and cook 30 seconds.

Mix together and cook 3 more minutes.

Stir in breadcrumbs. Serve with lemon wedges.
