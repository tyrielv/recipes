# Spanish Lentil & Sausage Soup

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/7331-hearty-spanish-style-lentil-and-chorizo-soup)*

## Ingredients

- Olive oil
- ~ 1 lb kielbasa, chopped
- 1 large onion, 3 Tbsp grated and the rest chopped
- 3 carrots, chopped
- 1 tsp dried marjoram
- 3 Tbsp red wine vinegar
- 2 tsp salt
- 1lb (2 1/4 C) brown lentils
- 7 C water (boil in kettle while sauteing for faster cook time)
- 2 bay leaves
- 1/8 tsp ground cloves
- 2 Tbsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 Tbsp flour

## Directions

Heat 2 Tbsp olive oil in instant pot on medium sauté setting. Add chopped kielbasa, onion, carrots, and marjoram, and sauté 10 minutes.

Stir in red wine vinegar and continue sauteing until most liquid has evaporated.

Add salt, lentils, water, bay leaves, and cloves. Pressure cook for 12 minutes with a quick release.

Meanwhile, in a small frying pan, saute 3 Tbsp olive oil with grated onion, paprika, black pepper, garlic, and flour for about 5 minutes, then remove from heat.

When pressure cooking is done, stir in the seasoning mix. Add additional salt to taste.
