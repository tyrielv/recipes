# Spicy Chicken Fritters

## Ingredients

- 3 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
- 6 green onions, chopped
- 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 Tbl curry paste (We used mild and it was plenty spicy for us)
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 1/2 cups bread crumbs
- Vegetable oil for frying

## Directions

Boil the potatoes until tender (10-15 minutes). Drain, then return to pan over medium heat for a minute to dry them off a bit.

Put in food processor (or bosch mixer with cookie paddles) with chicken, green onions, jalapeño, garlic, salt, and curry paste. Process until blended.

Transfer to a bowl. Add 1/2 cup bread crumbs and 2 Tbl of beaten egg and mix in.

Put the remaining egg and bread crumbs in separate shallow bowls. Form fritters out of the potato mixture, dip in egg and coat in bread crumbs, then fry for ~2 minutes on each side or until golden brown.

Serve with tartar sauce.
