# Spicy Sausage and Pasta Soup

*Adapted from <https://www.chelseasmessyapron.com/pasta-soup/>*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 pound spicy Italian sausage (mild sausage works too)**
- **Optional 1/4 tsp chili flakes if your Italian sausage isn't spicy.
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 3 sticks of celery, sliced
- 1-2 carrots, chopped
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 2 cans (15 ounces each) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
- 8 cups beef stock or broth
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- rounded 1/4 tsp ground rosemary
- rounded 1/4 tsp dried thyme
- rounded 1/4 tsp sage
- salt and pepper to taste
- 10 oz small shell pasta, uncooked (any smaller pasta would work)
- 4 cups (lightly packed when measured) fresh spinach, coarsely chopped
- Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

---

## Directions

**SAUSAGE:** Line a shallow bowl with paper towels and set aside. Heat a large (6 quart) cast iron stockpot over medium high heat. Add a drizzle of olive oil and once the oil is shimmering, add in the sausage (and chili flakes if desired) and brown, about 6-8 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside in bowl.

**VEGGIES:** Add in the onion, celery, and carrots. Sauté, stirring often, for 5-7 minutes or until tender. Add in garlic and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add in both cans of tomatoes and seasonings and allow to cook down for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

**PASTA:** Add broth and bring the soup to a boil. Add pasta. Stir and cook pasta to package directions. Stir in the cooked sausage and the spinach and stir until spinach wilts (only a minute or two). Remove from heat, and taste to adjust salt/pepper seasoning.

**SERVE:** Serve with Parmesan cheese and crusty bread. Enjoy!
