# Strawberry Compote

## Ingredients

- 12 oz strawberries, stemmed. (If using frozen, microwave on high 2-3 minutes to thaw)
- 3 Tbsp sugar or honey OR 1/4 tsp stevia extract
- 1 Tbsp water
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 Tbsp chia seeds, optionally ground if your kids are picky.

---

## Directions

Stir ingredients together in 2 qt sauce pan. Bring to a simmer over medium high. Reduce to medium low. Stir frequently and mash the strawberries as they soften. Cook until thickened, about 10-20 minutes depending on how cold the strawberries were.
