# Sweet and Sour Dipping Sauce

*From http://www.food.com/recipe/sweet-and-sour-dipping-sauce-175169*

I've been looking for a good sweet and sour dipping sauce for chicken nuggets for a while, and this is what I found:

## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 3 tbsp ketchup
- 3 tbsp water
- 2 tsp soy sauce (I used Teriyaki)
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 tsp corn flour

## Directions

Mix all ingredients together in a small sauce pan, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until thickened to desire. (Note: it will thicken slightly as it cools as well.)*

## Notes

*For future makings of this: I will probably use only 2.5 tbsp sugar because I use Teriyaki which already has sugar in it.

Otherwise, I really like it!
