# Sweet and Sour Meatballs

This is a recipe from a family cookbook. It is a favorite! We usually double the sauce recipe so that there is plenty to go with the rice.

## Ingredients

**Meatballs:**
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 lb ground beef
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1 tsp onion powder

**Sauce:**
- 1/4 c molasses
- 1/4 c ketchup
- 1/4 c vinegar
- 1/4 tsp hot sauce
- 1/4 tsp dried oregano

## Directions

Combine milk and bread crumbs in a bowl. Mix in beef, salt, and onion. Make meatballs and roll in flour to coat. Brown in a large skillet with vegetable oil.

Combine molasses, ketchup, vinegar, hot sauce, and oregano in a bowl and mix well. Add to skillet with meatballs and simmer for 20 minutes until glazed and cooked through.

Serve over rice or noodles, and enjoy!
