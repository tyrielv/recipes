# Sweet and Tangy Barbecue Sauce

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/5024-indoor-pulled-pork-with-sweet-and-tangy-barbecue-sauce)*

## Ingredients

- 6 Tbsp ketchup
- 1 Tbsp molasses
- 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 3/4 tsp hot sauce
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper
- 2 Tbsp defatted meat drippings

## Directions

Whisk together.
