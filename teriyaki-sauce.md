# Teriyaki Sauce

*Adapted from "The Best of America's Test Kitchen 2017"*

---

## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp cornstarch
- 1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
- 1/2 C mirin*
- 1/3 C water
- 1/4 C soy sauce
- 3 Tbsp sugar
- Optional 1 Tbsp sesame seeds for garnish

---

## Directions

Mix cornstarch and vinegar to a slurry and set aside.

Bring mirin, water, soy sauce, and sugar to a boil. Reduce heat, mix in slurry, and simmer for 1 minute. Remove from heat.

---

## Notes

*Mirin can be found at the regular grocery store in the Asian aisle with the sauces - it's mostly corn syrup with fermented rice extract). Rice vinegar can also substitute, though you'll need to counteract the sourness with about a 1/2 teaspoon of sugar for every tablespoon you use.
