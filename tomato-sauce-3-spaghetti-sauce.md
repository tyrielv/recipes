# Tomato Sauce #3 (Spaghetti Sauce)

*Adapted from "Classic Italian Cooking"*

---

## Ingredients

- 28 oz canned whole tomatoes
- 8 tbsp butter
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 1 medium onion, peeled and halved

---

## Directions

Heat all ingredients in a sauce pan over medium to a simmer. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 45 minutes. Remove and discard onion, then purée.
