# Tuna Noodle Salad

*Adapted from several different recipes in various places.*

## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp oil
- 1 rib celery, grated
- 1/2 small onion, grated
- 7 oz canned tuna
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/4 Cup roasted red bell pepper (optional)
- 8 oz pasta, cooked
- 1 additional Tbsp oil
- 2/3 Cup mayonnaise
- 2 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp dill
- 1/4 Cup chopped or slivered almonds (optional)

## Directions

Drain tuna in colander and press out extra water with paper towel.

Microwave 2 Tbsp oil with grated celery and onion 2 minutes. Allow to cool ~5 minutes. Mix with tuna, garlic, bell pepper, almonds.

Meanwhile cook pasta in salted water. Drain well and toss with 1 Tbsp oil to prevent sticking. Make dressing by stirring mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, and dill.

Stir tuna, pasta, and dressing together. Serve chilled.
