# Vegetable Broth Base

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/8204-vegetable-broth-base)*

## Ingredients

- 10 oz leeks (about 4)
- 6 oz carrots (about 4)
- 6 oz celery root (about 1 small)
- 6 Tbsp dry minced onion
- 4 Tbsp kosher salt
- 3 Tbsp tomato paste
- 6 Tbsp soy sauce

## Directions

Pureé leeks, carrots, celery root, and dry onion with salt in food processor or blender.

Thoroughly mix in tomato paste and soy sauce.

Store in freezer.

Mix 1 Tbsp of base with 1 C water to make vegetable broth.
