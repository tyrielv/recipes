# Vegetarian "Meat" Spaghetti Sauce

*Adapted from [America's Test Kitchen](https://www.americastestkitchen.com/recipes/9629-meatless-meat-sauce-with-chickpeas-and-mushrooms)*

## Ingredients

- 28-ounce canned crushed tomatoes
- 8 oz mushrooms
- 6 Tbsp olive oil, divided
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 onion
- 5 cloves minced garlic
- 1 1/4 tsp oregano
- 1 Tbsp basil
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/4 Cup tomato paste
- 2 Cups vegetable broth
- 15-ounce canned chickpeas

## Directions

Cook tomatoes in saucepan, covered, over medium heat while preparing the mushrooms and onions (20-30 minutes).

Chop mushrooms finely. Heat 5 Tbsp olive oil in large skillet over medium high. Add mushrooms and salt and cook, stirring occasionally, 10 minutes.

Chop onions finely. Add to mushrooms and cook 5 minutes.

Combine 1 Tbsp olive oil, garlic, oregano, basil, and pepper flakes in a small bowl.

Stir tomato paste to onions and mushrooms and cook 2 minutes. Stir in other seasonings. Stir in tomatoes and broth. Bring to simmer, then reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.

Lightly mash chickpeas, then rinse and drain in a mesh strainer. Stir chickpeas into sauce and simmer 15 minutes.
