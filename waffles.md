# Waffles

*Adapted from the instruction manual to our waffle maker*

## Ingredients

* 3 eggs
* 1 cup milk
* 1 stick melted butter
* 1 Tbl vanilla
* 2 C flour (optionally use whole wheat flour for half)
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 Tbl baking powder
* 2 tsp sugar

## Directions

Beat all ingredients together until smooth. Makes ~5 6-inch diameter waffles.

## Notes

For crisper waffles, beat the egg whites separately until stiff, then fold into batter.
