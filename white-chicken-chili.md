# White Chicken Chili

*Adapted from http://www.joshuakennon.com/make-favorite-white-chicken-chili-recipe/*

## Ingredients

* 1 Tbl oil
* 2 chicken breasts, chopped into 1/2 in. cubes
* 1 large onion, chopped
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 can chicken broth (1 3/4 C)
* 2 large green chilies (such as Anaheim or poblano), seeded and chopped
* 4 jalapeños (or 3 and a serrano for a spicier chili, or 2 more large green chilies for a milder chili), seeded and chopped
* 2 cans great northern beans, drained and rinsed (or 1.5 C dry white beans, cooked)
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp cumin
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1/2 tsp ground coriander
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, or 1/4 tsp paprika for a milder chili
* 1 C sour cream
* 1/2 C heavy cream
* (optional) 10 corn tortillas, chopped

## Directions

Sauté chicken, onion, and chilies in oil until chicken is no longer pink, about 10-15 minutes.

Add garlic and sauté for a few more seconds.

Add broth, cooked beans, and seasonings.

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for about 40 minutes.

Just before serving stir in sour cream, heavy cream, and (optional) tortillas.
